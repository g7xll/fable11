## STONES (STONEREVEAL COMPONENT, MOUNTED TWICE — LEFT AND RIGHT)

TWO PHOTOREALISTIC STONES ANCHORED TO THE BOTTOM CORNERS. EACH INSTANCE IS AN ABSOLUTELY-POSITIONED WRAPPER AT BOTTOM:0, WITH SIDE:0 (LEFT OR RIGHT), W-FIT, CURSOR-CROSSHAIR. HEIGHTS ARE RESPONSIVE: 280PX MOBILE / 380PX SM / 500PX MD / 600PX LG / 680PX XL.

EACH STONE IS COMPOSED OF TWO STACKED IMAGES:

BASE STONE (ZBASE: 1 FOR LEFT, 4 FOR RIGHT): THE DRY/CLEAN STONE PNG (STONE-LEFT.PNG / STONE-RIGHT.PNG).

GRASS OVERLAY (ZGRASS: 2 FOR LEFT, 5 FOR RIGHT): THE SAME STONE BUT COVERED IN MOSS/GRASS (STONE-G-LEFT.PNG / STONE-G-RIGHT.PNG), POSITIONED ABSOLUTELY ON TOP, POINTER-EVENTS NONE.

BOTH IMAGES USE OBJECT-FIT CONTAIN, OBJECT-POSITION ANCHORED TO THE CORRESPONDING BOTTOM EDGE ("LEFT BOTTOM" OR "RIGHT BOTTOM").

STONE ENTRY ANIMATION — BASE IMAGE FADES IN FROM OPACITY 0 WITH X-OFFSET (-40PX FOR LEFT, +40PX FOR RIGHT) TO OPACITY 1 / X:0 OVER 0.9S, EASEOUT, WITH A 0.5S DELAY.

MOSSY REVEAL INTERACTION (THE SIGNATURE MICRO-INTERACTION) — ON MOUSE ENTER, A RADIAL MASK REVEALS THE GRASS OVERLAY THROUGH A SOFT CIRCLE THAT FOLLOWS THE CURSOR:

A MOTION VALUE RADIUSRAW IS SET TO 120 ON MOUSEENTER AND BACK TO 0 ON MOUSELEAVE.

A SPRING (STIFFNESS 200, DAMPING 25) SMOOTHS IT INTO RADIUS.

THE GRASS IMAGE'S CSS MASK IS RADIAL-GRADIENT(CIRCLE ${RADIUS}PX AT ${X}PX ${Y}PX, BLACK 0%, BLACK 40%, TRANSPARENT 100%), WHERE X/Y ARE TRACKED FROM THE MOUSEMOVE EVENT RELATIVE TO THE WRAPPER'S BOUNDING RECT.

EFFECT: AS THE USER HOVERS, A SOFT CIRCULAR "WET" PATCH OF MOSS ORGANICALLY APPEARS UNDER THE CURSOR AND FADES OUT AT THE EDGES; ON LEAVE, IT SPRINGS CLOSED.
