      Speak with an Expert
      What levels of care do you provide?

      Almond Hours offers a full continuum of care — independent living, assisted living, memory excellence (dementia care), and short-term clinical rehabilitation — so residents transition seamlessly as their needs change.

      Can families visit at any time?

      Absolutely. Almond Hours is a resident's home. We encourage family involvement with no restrictive visiting hours, and offer guest suites and private dining rooms for gatherings or overnight stays.

      How do you handle medical emergencies?

      Every community has 24/7 on-site nursing and emergency response in every suite, direct lines to local medical centers, and real-time monitoring that detects issues before they become emergencies.

      What is your staff-to-resident ratio?

      We maintain one of the highest staffing ratios in the industry — higher still in Memory Excellence and Rehab — always prioritizing individualized attention and safety over operational metrics.

      Are pets allowed in the communities?

      Yes — we believe pets are family. Well-behaved companions are welcome in our Independent and Assisted Living programs, with concierge pet-care services for walking and grooming.
