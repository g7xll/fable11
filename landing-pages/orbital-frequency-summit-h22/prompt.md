  SLOWLY ROTATING RADAR SWEEP, MONOSPACE TELEMETRY LABELS (COORDINATES, FREQUENCIES,

## MOTION / ANIMATION / INTERACTION SPEC

- HERO ENTRANCE: STATUS PILL, TITLE LINES, TELEMETRY STRIP, AND CTA FADE/RISE IN
  WITH STAGGERED DELAYS.
- CONTINUOUS AMBIENT MOTION: ORBITAL RINGS ROTATE (15S–40S, ALTERNATING
  DIRECTIONS), RADAR CONE SWEEPS (~6S LINEAR), GREEN "LIVE" DOTS PULSE, STAR
  PARTICLES DRIFT, AND THE PASS BEACONS BOB.
- SCROLL REVEALS: SECTION BLOCKS AND CARDS FADE + RISE VIA AN INTERSECTION
  OBSERVER, WITH A SUBTLE STAGGER ACROSS GRID ITEMS.
- LIVE COUNTERS IN MISSION COUNT UP WHEN SCROLLED INTO VIEW.
- HOVER STATES: TRACK CARDS LIFT + GLOW + GROW THEIR UNDERLINE; CTA BUTTONS FILL
  FROM ONE EDGE AND EXPAND CORNER BRACKETS; SPEAKER PORTRAITS COLORIZE + ZOOM; FAQ
  "+" ROTATES TO "×".
- A THIN PROGRESS / SIGNAL BAR AT THE VERY TOP TRACKS SCROLL DEPTH IN AMBER.
- RESPECT `PREFERS-REDUCED-MOTION`: DISABLE THE LOOPING AMBIENT ANIMATIONS AND
  REVEAL ALL CONTENT IMMEDIATELY.
