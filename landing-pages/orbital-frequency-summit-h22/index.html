FREQUENTLY ASKED

Do I need a space background?
No. If you can write firmware, decode a signal, push pixels, or reason about a system, there's a seat for you. Crews are deliberately mixed — the best builds come from blended skill sets.
Do I arrive with a team?
Solo is welcome. Friday's crew-formation window pairs you with people on the same orbit. Pre-formed crews of up to five are also fine.
Is the telemetry real?
Yes — you work against archived and simulated passes from real ground stations, with authentic link budgets and frame formats. No fictional toy data.
What should I bring?
A laptop, your dev environment, and a charger. SDR dongles, antennas, and lab hardware are provided on-site; bring your own if you have a favorite.
