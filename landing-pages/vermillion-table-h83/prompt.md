# VERMILLION TABLE — ARCHITECTURAL PREMIUM FOOD-DELIVERY LANDING PAGE

BUILD A FULL, MULTI-SECTION, FULLY RESPONSIVE MARKETING LANDING PAGE FOR A FICTIONAL HIGH-END FOOD-DELIVERY SERVICE NAMED **VERMILLION TABLE** — "TASTE BEYOND BOUNDARIES." THE NAMED AESTHETIC IDENTITY IS **"CULINARY ARCHITECTURE"**: A WARM, EDITORIAL, GALLERY-LIKE LOOK THAT TREATS FOOD DELIVERY LIKE A DESIGN OBJECT — VANILLA-PAPER BACKGROUNDS, ROUNDED MODULAR "ISLAND" CARDS, OVERSIZED ULTRA-TIGHT DISPLAY TYPE, AND A CONFIDENT RED-ORANGE / DEEP-TEAL DUOTONE. THE FEEL IS A NEIGHBORHOOD ATELIER MENU CROSSED WITH A SWISS DESIGN MAGAZINE: APPETIZING AND TRUSTWORTHY, NEVER GLOSSY-CORPORATE OR GENERIC-SAAS.

## DESIGN LANGUAGE & MOOD

- WARM, INTENTIONAL, ARCHITECTURAL. EVERYTHING IS A ROUNDED "ISLAND" PANEL SITTING ON A PALE VANILLA CANVAS WITH GENEROUS GUTTERS AND VERTICAL RHYTHM (CONSISTENT 8-UNIT SPACE BETWEEN ISLANDS).
- MAX CONTENT WIDTH ~1446PX, CENTERED, WITH COMFORTABLE SIDE PADDING ON MOBILE.
- HEAVY USE OF NEGATIVE SPACE, OVERSIZED HEADLINES WITH EXTREME NEGATIVE LETTER-SPACING (TIGHTER-EXTRA), AND SMALL UPPERCASE WIDE-TRACKED LABELS AS COUNTERPOINT.
- NO HARSH BORDERS EXCEPT SOFT HAIRLINES; SHADOWS ARE SOFT AND DIFFUSE. ROUNDED CORNERS EVERYWHERE (20PX ISLANDS, 30PX FEATURE IMAGES, PILL/2XL BUTTONS).

## HERO COMPOSITION

THE HERO IS TWO STACKED ISLANDS: A TEXT ISLAND WHERE THE OVERSIZED HEADLINE IS BASELINE-ANCHORED TO THE BOTTOM AND THE CTA SITS RIGHT, FOLLOWED IMMEDIATELY BY THE FULL-BLEED IMAGE ISLAND WITH ITS FLOATING FROSTED INFO CARD. THE TWO READ AS A SINGLE EDITORIAL SPREAD.

## MOTION / ANIMATION / INTERACTION SPEC

- SCROLL-REVEAL: ELEMENTS START AT OPACITY 0 / TRANSLATEY(20PX) AND EASE TO VISIBLE WHEN THEY ENTER THE VIEWPORT (INTERSECTION OBSERVER, ~0.8S EASE-OUT, ONE-SHOT).
- PHONE MOCKUP: INFINITE GENTLE VERTICAL FLOAT (~6S EASE-IN-OUT, ±10PX).
- CATEGORY CARDS: IMAGE SCALE TO ~1.1 OVER ~0.7S AND OVERLAY TINT SHIFTS TEAL→RED-ORANGE ON HOVER.
- BUTTONS: HOVER TO BLACK BACKGROUND / WHITE TEXT, ACTIVE SCALE 0.95, FAST 150MS TRANSITIONS.
- SMOOTH-SCROLL FOR IN-PAGE ANCHOR LINKS WITH A FIXED-HEADER OFFSET.
- RESPECT `PREFERS-REDUCED-MOTION`.
