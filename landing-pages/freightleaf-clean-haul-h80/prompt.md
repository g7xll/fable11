## AESTHETIC IDENTITY

LOGISTICS COMPANY NAMED **FREIGHTLEAF**. THE NAMED AESTHETIC IS **"CLEAN HAUL"** —
A MODERN, OPTIMISTIC, ECO-FORWARD EDITORIAL LANGUAGE THAT PAIRS A WARM NEUTRAL
PAPER-GRAY CANVAS WITH DEEP CHARCOAL "NIGHT HIGHWAY" PANELS AND ONE ELECTRIC,
BIO-LUMINESCENT LIME-GREEN ACCENT. THE MOOD IS CALM CONFIDENCE: ENGINEERED,
PREMIUM, AND ENVIRONMENTAL WITHOUT LOOKING LIKE GENERIC "GREENWASHED" CORPORATE
SLOP. THINK SWISS-ADJACENT GRID DISCIPLINE, GENEROUS WHITESPACE, OVERSIZED PILL
SHAPES, AND LARGE SOFT-ROUNDED PANELS THAT FEEL LIKE CARGO MODULES.

## MOTION / INTERACTION SPEC

- SCROLL-REVEAL: ELEMENTS FADE+SLIDE IN VIA INTERSECTIONOBSERVER WITH DIRECTIONAL
  VARIANTS — UP (TRANSLATEY 30PX), LEFT (TRANSLATEX -60PX), RIGHT (TRANSLATEX
  60PX), AND SCALE (FROM 1.1). EASING `cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1)`, DURATION
  1–1.5S, WITH PER-ELEMENT STAGGER DELAYS (0.1S / 0.2S / 0.3S).
- HERO BACKGROUND IMAGE SLOWLY SCALES IN (KEN BURNS).
- THE HERO STAT CARD CONTINUOUSLY FLOATS (≈5S EASE-IN-OUT, ±15PX) VIA A SEPARATE
  INNER WRAPPER SO THE FLOAT DOESN'T FIGHT THE ENTRY TRANSFORM.
- TESTIMONIAL TICKER: LINEAR INFINITE TRANSLATEX MARQUEE (≈30S), PAUSE ON HOVER.
- DARK-SECTION METRICS COUNT UP FROM ZERO WHEN SCROLLED INTO VIEW.
- BUTTONS: SMOOTH OPACITY/SCALE HOVERS; LINKS UNDERLINE OR SHIFT THE LIME ARROW.
- RESPECT `prefers-reduced-motion`: DISABLE FLOAT, KEN-BURNS, AND COUNT-UP MOTION
  AND SHOW FINAL STATES.

## RESPONSIVE BEHAVIOR

- DESKTOP: HERO IS A TWO-COLUMN BOTTOM-ALIGNED COMPOSITION; SECTORS ARE 3 COLUMNS;
  TECH SECTION IS A 2-COLUMN SPLIT.
- TABLET/MOBILE: STACK EVERYTHING TO ONE COLUMN, CENTER THE HERO STAT CARD UNDER
  THE COPY, COLLAPSE NAV TO THE HAMBURGER SHEET, REDUCE TYPE SCALE, AND KEEP
  COMFORTABLE TAP TARGETS. NO HORIZONTAL OVERFLOW EXCEPT THE INTENTIONAL TICKER.
