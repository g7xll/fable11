# EMBER QUIETUDE — A CALM PRODUCTIVITY WORKSPACE LANDING PAGE

## AESTHETIC IDENTITY

BUILD A FULL, MULTI-SECTION MARKETING LANDING PAGE FOR A FICTIONAL FOCUS-FIRST PRODUCTIVITY APP CALLED **LUMO** — "A QUIET PLACE FOR A LOUD MIND." THE NAMED DESIGN LANGUAGE IS **EMBER QUIETUDE**: A WARM, PAPER-CALM EDITORIAL AESTHETIC THAT FEELS LIKE A WELL-LIT STUDY DESK AT GOLDEN HOUR. THE MOOD IS SERENE, CONFIDENT, AND HUMAN — THE OPPOSITE OF THE COLD, NEON SAAS DASHBOARD. WARM NEUTRAL STONE PAPER, A SINGLE EMBER-ORANGE ACCENT, GENEROUS WHITESPACE, AND A LARGE ITALIC DISPLAY SERIF THAT MAKES THE PRODUCT FEEL THOUGHTFUL RATHER THAN TECHY. ABSOLUTELY NO GENERIC-AI-SLOP: NO PURPLE-BLUE GRADIENTS AS THE PRIMARY THEME, NO STOCK HERO PHOTOS, NO TEMPLATED CARD GRIDS WITHOUT CHARACTER.

## COLOR PALETTE (EXACT)

- PAPER BACKGROUND: `#FAFAF8` (WARM STONE-50), SECONDARY PANELS `#F5F4F1`.
- INK / NEAR-BLACK: `#1C1917` (STONE-900) FOR TEXT AND DARK PANELS.
- MUTED INK: `#78716C` (STONE-500) FOR SUBHEADS AND BODY.
- HAIRLINE BORDERS: `#E7E5E4` (STONE-200).
- EMBER ACCENT (PRIMARY): `#EA580C` (ORANGE-600), HOVER `#C2410C`, SOFT WASH `#FFEDD5`.
- SUPPORTING UI DOT COLORS USED SPARINGLY INSIDE MOCKUPS ONLY: BLUE `#3B82F6`, PURPLE `#8B5CF6`, GREEN `#22C55E`, AMBER `#F59E0B`, RED `#EF4444`.
- SELECTION HIGHLIGHT: EMBER WASH BACKGROUND WITH EMBER TEXT.

## TYPOGRAPHY

- DISPLAY / SERIF: **INSTRUMENT SERIF** (REGULAR + ITALIC) — USE FOR ALL HEADLINES, THE LOGOTYPE, AND THE PRODUCT MOCKUP TITLES. HEADLINES ARE LARGE (CLAMP UP TO ~7-8REM ON HERO), TIGHT LEADING (~0.95), WITH KEY WORDS SET IN ITALIC AND IN MUTED STONE-500 FOR EMPHASIS.
- SANS / UI: **INTER** (WEIGHTS 300/400/500/600/700) — FOR BODY, NAV, BUTTONS, LABELS, AND ALL MOCKUP UI TEXT.
- MICRO-LABELS: UPPERCASE, 10–11PX, LETTER-SPACING ~0.2EM, BOLD, IN EITHER STONE-400 OR EMBER.
- VENDOR BOTH FONTS LOCALLY AS WOFF2 (SELF-CONTAINED, OFFLINE-CAPABLE) WITH `@font-face`; DO NOT HOTLINK GOOGLE FONTS.

## MOTION / ANIMATION / INTERACTION SPEC

- SCROLL-REVEAL: SECTIONS AND KEY ELEMENTS FADE-AND-RISE INTO VIEW VIA AN INTERSECTIONOBSERVER (STAGGERED DELAYS), TRIGGERED ONCE, RESPECTING `prefers-reduced-motion`.
- FLOATING ELEMENTS: THE HERO GLOW, THE "AI BRIEFING" CARD, AND THE SLASH-MENU CARD GENTLY BOB WITH A 6S EASE-IN-OUT FLOAT KEYFRAME.
- FOCUS-TIMER PROGRESS BAR HAS A SUBTLE LEFT-TO-RIGHT SHIMMER SWEEP.
- BUTTONS: EMBER/INK BUTTONS LIFT SLIGHTLY AND DEEPEN SHADOW ON HOVER; ARROWS TRANSLATE RIGHT; ACTIVE STATE SCALES DOWN 0.97.
- NAVBAR: HOVERING THE HEADER REVEALS THE MEGA-MENU DRAWER WITH AN OPACITY+TRANSLATE TRANSITION; THE LOGO MARK ROTATES 360° ON HOVER. NAVBAR GAINS A STRONGER SHADOW AFTER SCROLLING PAST THE HERO.
- A "BACK TO TOP" CIRCULAR INK BUTTON FADES IN AFTER SCROLLING ~400PX AND SMOOTH-SCROLLS TO TOP.
- THE LIVE BADGE DOT PINGS CONTINUOUSLY. SMOOTH ANCHOR SCROLLING FOR NAV LINKS.

## RESPONSIVE BEHAVIOR

- DESKTOP (≥1024PX): FULL FLOATING PILL NAV WITH MEGA-MENU, MULTI-COLUMN HERO MOCKUP WITH SIDEBAR AND FLOATING CARD, MULTI-COLUMN BENTO/PRICING GRIDS.
- TABLET: COLLAPSE BENTO AND FEATURE GRIDS TO ONE OR TWO COLUMNS; HERO MOCKUP HIDES ITS LEAST-IMPORTANT COLUMNS.
- MOBILE (<768PX): COMPACT TOP PILL + HAMBURGER → FULL-SCREEN PAPER OVERLAY MENU WITH LARGE SERIF LINKS; HERO HEADLINE SCALES DOWN; MOCKUP SIDEBAR AND FLOATING CARD HIDE; ALL GRIDS STACK; TAP TARGETS ≥44PX. NO HORIZONTAL OVERFLOW AT ANY WIDTH.
