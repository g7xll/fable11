Talk to an expert
Do you accept insurance and cashless billing?

Yes — we partner with all major insurers and TPAs. Our billing desk handles smooth cashless processing for your delivery so you can focus on your baby.

What exactly is an LDRP suite?

LDRP stands for Labour, Delivery, Recovery and Postpartum. You stay in one calm, private suite for your entire birthing journey — maximum comfort, minimum disruption.

Is a neonatologist available around the clock?

Always. Lumina runs a dedicated Level III NICU with neonatologists and specialised nursing staff on-site 24/7 to respond to any newborn need instantly.

Can my partner stay with me in the room?

Absolutely. Every LDRP and luxury suite comfortably accommodates one companion. We believe deeply in family-centred care throughout the entire process.
