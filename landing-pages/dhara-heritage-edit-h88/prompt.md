## MOTION / ANIMATION / INTERACTION SPEC

- **HERO SLIDESHOW**: CROSS-FADE BETWEEN 3 IMAGES, ~1.5S EASE, ADVANCING EVERY 5S, LOOPING.
- **SCROLL REVEALS**: EACH MAJOR SECTION FADES + RISES (OPACITY 0→1, TRANSLATE-Y 20PX→0, ~0.8S EASE-OUT) AS IT ENTERS THE VIEWPORT VIA INTERSECTIONOBSERVER; EACH TRIGGERS ONCE.
- **HOVER**: CATEGORY/PRODUCT IMAGES DESATURATE→SATURATE AND SCALE (~1.05) OVER 0.7–1S; QUICK-ADD BARS SLIDE UP + FADE IN; CTA ARROWS NUDGE UP-RIGHT; UNDERLINE LINKS SHIFT ACCENT COLOR.
- **MOBILE MENU**: FULL-SCREEN PANEL SLIDES IN FROM THE RIGHT (~0.5S), LOCKS BODY SCROLL, AND CLOSES ON LINK TAP OR THE CLOSE ICON.
- SMOOTH SCROLL ON ANCHOR LINKS; A THIN CUSTOM SCROLLBAR (PINE-GREEN THUMB).
