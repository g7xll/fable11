THE NAMED DESIGN LANGUAGE IS **"BRUTALIST RETAIL EDITORIAL"**: THE COLLISION OF A RAW, CONCRETE-AND-INK BRUTALIST POSTER WITH THE LOUD, CONVERSION-HUNGRY ENERGY OF A MODERN INDIAN SHOPPING APP. STRICTLY AVOID GENERIC-AI-SLOP: NO PURPLE-BLUE GRADIENTS, NO GLASSMORPHISM BLUR CARDS AS THE WHOLE THEME, NO NEON CYBERPUNK, NO EMOJI. THE ENERGY COMES FROM ONE DISCIPLINED ORANGE, OVERSIZED TIGHT-TRACKED UPPERCASE TYPE, NEAR-ZERO BORDER RADIUS, HARD HAIRLINE BORDERS, AND SCROLLING MARQUEES.

- **CONCRETE BEIGE (PAGE BG):** `RGB(232, 229, 226)` — A WARM, PAPER-LIKE OFF-WHITE STONE.

- **VERMILION ORANGE (ACCENT):** `#FE5733` — THE ONLY SATURATED COLOR; USED FOR HEADLINE HIGHLIGHT WORDS, BADGES, ICONS, PRICES, AND HOVER STATES. USE SPARINGLY.

- **TEXT SECONDARY:** INK AT 60–70% OPACITY FOR BODY COPY; INK AT 40% FOR META / STRUCK-THROUGH PRICES.
- **WHATSAPP GREEN:** `#25D366` — RESERVED ONLY FOR THE FLOATING WHATSAPP FAB.

## MOTION / ANIMATION / INTERACTION SPEC

- **SCROLL REVEAL:** ELEMENTS TAGGED FOR ANIMATION START AT `OPACITY 0` / `TRANSLATEY(20PX)` AND EASE TO VISIBLE VIA AN INTERSECTIONOBSERVER (THRESHOLD ~0.05), STAGGERED PER SECTION.
- **MARQUEES:** TWO CONTINUOUS, GPU-FRIENDLY HORIZONTAL MARQUEES (CATEGORY + STATS) USING A `TRANSLATEX(0 → -50%)` KEYFRAME WITH A DUPLICATED TRACK FOR SEAMLESS LOOPING (~40S LINEAR INFINITE).
- **PRODUCT CARDS:** IMAGE `SCALE(1.1)` ON HOVER OVER 0.7S; THE "QUICK ADD TO CART" BUTTON SLIDES UP AND FADES IN ON HOVER.
- **ADD TO CART:** CLICK INCREMENTS THE NAV CART BADGE COUNT AND TEMPORARILY SWAPS THE BUTTON LABEL TO "ADDED ✓" BEFORE REVERTING.
- **MOBILE MENU:** SLIDES UP FROM BELOW (`TRANSLATEY(100%) → 0`) OVER ~0.5S AND LOCKS BODY SCROLL WHILE OPEN.
- **HOVER STATES:** INK BUTTONS → ORANGE; OUTLINE BUTTONS → FILL; LINKS → ORANGE; OFFER CARDS → ORANGE BORDER.
- **PINCODE / NEWSLETTER:** PURELY CLIENT-SIDE FAKE SUBMITS WITH INLINE FEEDBACK; NO BACKEND.
- RESPECT `PREFERS-REDUCED-MOTION` BY DISABLING NON-ESSENTIAL TRANSITIONS/MARQUEES.
