✦ Why Terra Siena

Industrial precision, domestic soul

Local Stoneware

Dug From the Hill

Support & Care

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about our clay, glazes, shipping and the care of hand-fired stoneware.

Still have questions? atelier@terrasiena.studio

We use a single high-iron stoneware dug from the Itria valley, glazed with hand-mixed wood-ash and mineral-oxide glazes. Every glaze is lead-free, food-safe and fired to full vitrification at 1280°C.

Yes. Because every piece is fired to a high temperature, our stoneware is oven, microwave and dishwasher safe. We simply ask that you avoid sudden thermal shock — no fridge straight to oven.

In-stock pieces ship within 2–3 business days. Hand-thrown commissions and limited firings take 4–6 weeks to throw, dry, bisque and glaze-fire. You'll receive a personal timeline at confirmation.

Wood firing is unpredictable by nature — flame, ash and placement in the kiln leave their own marks. Slight differences in tone, glaze pooling and form are intentional and celebrated, never flaws.

Absolutely. We work with restaurants, stylists and interior studios worldwide. Trade members receive preferential pricing, priority firings and dedicated support for larger commissions.
