## SIGNATURE MOTIFS (USE THROUGHOUT)

- **PLUS CROSSHAIR MARKERS**: A 15×15PX "+" DRAWN WITH TWO 3PX-THICK BARS, PINNED HALF-OUTSIDE THE CORNERS OF KEY FRAMES (NAV BAR, HERO SECTION, CARDS, CTA, FOOTER) — ALWAYS OFFSET BY `-8PX` SO THEY STRADDLE THE BORDER LIKE REGISTRATION CROSSHAIRS. ON THE GREEN CTA THEY TURN CREAM.
- **HAIRLINE CELL GRID**: SECTIONS AND NAV ITEMS ARE DIVIDED BY 1PX HAIRLINE BORDERS; THE WHOLE PAGE READS AS A STACK OF RULED FRAMES.
- **TEXT FLIP-UP HOVER**: NAV LINKS AND MENU-LIST LINKS REVEAL A GREEN FILL THAT SLIDES UP FROM THE BOTTOM WHILE THE LABEL TRANSLATES OUT AND A CREAM DUPLICATE SLIDES IN (OVERFLOW-HIDDEN "FLIP" EFFECT).

## MOTION / INTERACTION SPEC

- **SCROLL REVEAL**: ELEMENTS TAGGED FOR ANIMATION START HIDDEN (OPACITY 0, TRANSLATED 30PX UP, OR ±100PX FROM LEFT/RIGHT FOR HERO COLUMNS) AND EASE INTO PLACE VIA INTERSECTIONOBSERVER WITH A `CUBIC-BEZIER(0.16,1,0.3,1)` ~1.2S TRANSITION; STAGGER THE TWO HERO COLUMNS (300MS / 600MS DELAY).
- **STAT COUNT-UP**: ANIMATE FROM 0 TO TARGET (SUPPORTING DECIMALS) OVER ~2S USING `REQUESTANIMATIONFRAME`, TRIGGERED ONCE WHEN THE IMPACT SECTION ENTERS VIEW.
- **NAV / LINK FLIP-UP**: 500MS EASE TRANSFORM REVEALING THE GREEN-FILLED DUPLICATE LABEL.
- **MEGA MENU**: FADE + 8PX TRANSLATE IN ON TRIGGER HOVER, CONTENT SWAPPED BY DATA KEY, CLOSE ON WRAPPER MOUSE-LEAVE.
- **MARQUEE**: 30S LINEAR INFINITE LOOP, SEAMLESS.
- **CTA ARROW NUDGE, FAQ ICON ROTATE, IMAGE/LINK MICRO-HOVERS** AS DESCRIBED.
