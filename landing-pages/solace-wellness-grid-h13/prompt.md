# SOLACE — HOLISTIC WELLNESS EDITORIAL LANDING PAGE

## AESTHETIC IDENTITY

BUILD A SINGLE-PAGE MARKETING LANDING SITE FOR **"SOLACE"**, A MODERN HOLISTIC WELLNESS STUDIO. THE NAMED AESTHETIC IS **"QUIET LUXURY WELLNESS"**: A WARM, SUN-WASHED, EDITORIAL MAGAZINE FEEL BUILT ON AN ASYMMETRIC BENTO COLOR-BLOCK GRID. THE MOOD IS CALM, GROUNDED, EXPENSIVE, AND ORGANIC — LIKE A PRINTED SPA BROCHURE CROSSED WITH A SWISS EDITORIAL LAYOUT. NOTHING SHOUTS; EVERYTHING BREATHES. AVOID GENERIC SAAS GRADIENTS, GLASSMORPHISM, AND NEON. THE DESIGN LANGUAGE LIVES IN FLAT, MATTE COLOR PANELS THAT BUTT AGAINST EACH OTHER WITH HAIRLINE GUTTERS, OVERSIZED LIGHT-WEIGHT DISPLAY TYPE, AND GENEROUS NEGATIVE SPACE.

## COLOR SYSTEM

USE A WARM, MUTED, EARTHY PALETTE OF MATTE PANEL FILLS — NO PURE BLACK, NO PURE SATURATED COLOR:

- BASE / CANVAS: WARM OFF-WHITE PORCELAIN `#FDFCFB`
- CREAM / OAT PANEL: `#F2EEE3`
- DUSTY ROSE / CLAY PANEL: `#E6D5D1`
- LILAC MIST PANEL: `#E7E1EC`
- SAGE / FOREST ACCENT (BUTTONS, INK): `#4B594D`
- DEEPER MOSS HOVER: `#3C4A3E`
- PRIMARY INK / TEXT: `#3D3D3D`
- LIGHT TEXT (ON SAGE): `#FFFFFF`
- HAIRLINE BORDERS: INK AT 5–8% OPACITY

PANELS ARE FLAT FILLS WITH SUBTLE 1–2PX HAIRLINE GUTTERS BETWEEN THEM (THE CANVAS SHOWS THROUGH). NO DROP SHADOWS ON PANELS. IMAGES ARE SLIGHTLY DESATURATED (GRAYSCALE ~20%) BY DEFAULT AND RETURN TO FULL COLOR ON HOVER OVER A SLOW 1S TRANSITION.

## TYPOGRAPHY

- DISPLAY / PRIMARY: **MANROPE** (WEIGHTS 400, 500, 600, 700) — USED LIGHT AND OVERSIZED FOR HEADLINES.
- SECONDARY / UI LABELS: **INTER TIGHT** (400, 500, 600).
- HEADLINES: VERY LARGE (`clamp(56px, 9vw, 118px)`), WEIGHT 400, TIGHT NEGATIVE TRACKING (`-0.03em`), LINE-HEIGHT ~0.95.
- EYEBROWS / NAV / LABELS: 11PX, UPPERCASE, WEIGHT 600, WIDE LETTER-SPACING (`0.15–0.25em`), INK AT 60% OPACITY.
- BODY: 16–23PX, WEIGHT 400, LINE-HEIGHT 1.3–1.6, CONSTRAINED MEASURE (MAX ~240–460PX).
- BIG STAT NUMERALS: `clamp(80px, 12vw, 120px)`, WEIGHT 400, TIGHT TRACKING.

## LAYOUT & SECTION BREAKDOWN

A FULL MULTI-SECTION LANDING PAGE (NOT JUST A HERO), CENTERED IN A MAX-WIDTH ~1505PX CONTAINER WITH ~30PX OUTER PADDING ON DESKTOP AND ~10PX GUTTERS BETWEEN PANELS.

1. **STICKY HEADER** — TRANSLUCENT CANVAS WITH BACKDROP BLUR, HAIRLINE BOTTOM BORDER. LEFT: WORDMARK "SOLACE" IN WIDE UPPERCASE TRACKING + DESKTOP NAV (PHILOSOPHY, PRACTICES, JOURNAL). RIGHT: "RESERVE SPACE" UNDERLINED SAGE LINK + A MOBILE HAMBURGER. MOBILE OPENS A FULL-SCREEN SLIDE-IN OVERLAY MENU.

2. **HERO (THE BENTO GRID)** — THE SIGNATURE COMPOSITION. A FULL-WIDTH SLIGHTLY-DESATURATED IMAGE STRIP (~400PX TALL) ACROSS THE TOP, THEN BELOW IT A BOTTOM ROW SPLIT INTO THREE ASYMMETRIC PANELS WITH A `740fr 380fr 324fr` COLUMN RATIO ON DESKTOP (STACKED ON MOBILE), MIN-HEIGHT ~430PX:
   - PANEL A (DUSTY ROSE, WIDEST): THE OVERSIZED HEADLINE "RECLAIM YOUR NATURAL VITALITY".
   - PANEL B (CREAM): A SHORT PHILOSOPHY LINE AT TOP ("HOLISTIC PRACTICES TAILORED TO YOUR MODERN LIFESTYLE") AND, ANCHORED TO THE BOTTOM, A FULL-WIDTH SAGE CTA BAR "JOIN A SESSION" WITH A DIAGONAL ARROW GLYPH THAT NUDGES UP-RIGHT ON HOVER.
   - PANEL C (LILAC MIST): A LARGE IMPACT STAT "94%" WITH CAPTION "REPORTED STRESS REDUCTION", NUMBER TOP-ALIGNED AND CAPTION BOTTOM-ALIGNED.

3. **MARQUEE / TICKER** — A THIN HORIZONTAL SCROLLING STRIP OF WELLNESS KEYWORDS (BREATHWORK · SOUND BATHS · MOBILITY · MINDFULNESS · NUTRITION) SEPARATED BY SMALL GLYPHS, LOOPING SLOWLY.

4. **PHILOSOPHY** — TWO-COLUMN EDITORIAL: A LEFT EYEBROW + LARGE STATEMENT HEADLINE, A RIGHT BODY PARAGRAPH. CALM, GENEROUS WHITESPACE.

5. **PRACTICES** — A BENTO GRID OF 4–6 PRACTICE CARDS (E.G. BREATHWORK, SOUND THERAPY, MOVEMENT, NUTRITION COACHING), EACH A MATTE PANEL ROTATING THROUGH THE PALETTE, WITH A NUMBER INDEX, TITLE, SHORT BLURB, AND A SMALL ARROW. ONE CARD IS AN IMAGE TILE. CARDS LIFT/REVEAL ON SCROLL.

6. **STATS BAND** — A SAGE FULL-WIDTH BAND WITH 3–4 LARGE NUMERALS IN LIGHT TEXT (E.G. 12 YEARS, 8K MEMBERS, 40 GUIDES, 94% CALM).

7. **PRACTITIONERS / GUIDES** — A ROW OF GUIDE PORTRAIT CARDS (DESATURATED → COLOR ON HOVER) WITH NAME + DISCIPLINE.

8. **RESERVE / CTA SECTION** — A LARGE CLOSING PANEL WITH A SHORT FORM (NAME, EMAIL, A "JOIN" SUBMIT) OR A PROMINENT "RESERVE YOUR SPACE" CTA, SET ON A CREAM OR ROSE PANEL.

9. **FOOTER** — WORDMARK, COLUMNED LINKS, A FINE PRINT LINE, ALL IN MUTED INK ON CANVAS.

## MOTION / ANIMATION / INTERACTION

- SCROLL-REVEAL: SECTIONS AND CARDS FADE UP (`opacity 0→1`, `translateY 20px→0`) OVER ~0.8S EASE-OUT, TRIGGERED VIA INTERSECTIONOBSERVER WITH STAGGER.
- IMAGE HOVER: GRAYSCALE → FULL COLOR OVER 1S.
- CTA ARROW: TRANSLATES UP-RIGHT ON HOVER; SAGE BUTTONS BRIGHTEN/DEEPEN SLIGHTLY.
- MARQUEE: CONTINUOUS CSS KEYFRAME LOOP, PAUSE ON HOVER.
- HEADER: SUBTLE BACKDROP BLUR; STICKY.
- MOBILE MENU: FULL-SCREEN PANEL SLIDING IN FROM THE RIGHT (`translate-x-full` → `0`), 0.5S EASE.
- KEEP MOTION SUBTLE AND SLOW — IT SHOULD FEEL MEDITATIVE, NOT BOUNCY.

## RESPONSIVE BEHAVIOR

- DESKTOP: ASYMMETRIC MULTI-COLUMN BENTO GRIDS WITH HAIRLINE GUTTERS AND OUTER PADDING.
- TABLET: REDUCE COLUMN COUNTS; PRESERVE PANEL RHYTHM.
- MOBILE: PANELS STACK TO A SINGLE COLUMN, GUTTERS COLLAPSE TO EDGE-TO-EDGE, HEADLINE SCALES DOWN VIA `clamp()`, NAV COLLAPSES TO THE HAMBURGER OVERLAY. EVERYTHING REMAINS LEGIBLE AND TOUCH-FRIENDLY.
