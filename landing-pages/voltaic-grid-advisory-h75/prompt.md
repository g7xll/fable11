# VOLTAIC — A "PUTTY & VOLTAGE" STRATEGIC ADVISORY LANDING PAGE

## NAMED AESTHETIC IDENTITY

**VOLTAIC ADVISORY** — A FULL, MULTI-SECTION MARKETING LANDING PAGE FOR A FICTIONAL HIGH-END STRATEGY CONSULTANCY. THE DESIGN LANGUAGE IS **"PUTTY & VOLTAGE"**: A WARM, EXPENSIVE PUTTY-GREY CANVAS, GENEROUSLY ROUNDED CARD ARCHITECTURE, A NEUTRAL GROTESQUE DISPLAY FACE, AND ONE DECISIVE ELECTRIC-LIME ACCENT THAT DOES ALL THE EMOTIONAL WORK. THE PAGE IS BUILT FROM A STACK OF LARGE ROUNDED PANELS — EACH ITS OWN "TILE" — THAT FLOAT ON THE PUTTY BACKGROUND WITH GENEROUS PADDING AND SOFT NEGATIVE SPACE. THE MOOD IS COMPOSED, CONFIDENT, AND PREMIUM: CALM SWISS RESTRAINT PUNCTURED BY VOLTAGE-LIME AND A FAMILY OF SOFT PASTEL ACCENT CARDS. NOT FLASHY SAAS, NOT DARK-MODE NEON — WARM DAYLIGHT MINIMALISM WITH ONE LIVE WIRE.

## DESIGN LANGUAGE & MOOD

- TILE ARCHITECTURE: EVERY MAJOR SECTION IS A SINGLE ROUNDED PANEL (`BORDER-RADIUS ~20PX`) WITH LARGE INTERNAL PADDING (UP TO 52PX), CENTERED IN A MAX-WIDTH SHELL (~1392PX) WITH SMALL OUTER GUTTERS. PANELS ALTERNATE BETWEEN PURE WHITE, A WARM OFF-WHITE SURFACE, AND PASTEL ACCENT FILLS.
- ONE ACCENT DOES THE WORK: ELECTRIC LIME. USED ONLY ON THE PRIMARY CTA, A SINGLE HERO PANEL FILL, AND SMALL HOVER STATES. EVERYTHING ELSE IS BLACK INK, WARM GREYS, AND PASTEL TINTS.
- A FAMILY OF SOFT PASTELS (PALE YELLOW, LAVENDER, BLUSH PINK, MINT GREEN) IS USED FOR THE STACKING SERVICE CARDS AND BLOG TILES SO THE PAGE FEELS PLAYFUL BUT STILL EXPENSIVE.
- ALL UPPERCASE EYEBROW LABELS WITH WIDE TRACKING (~0.12EM) INTRODUCE EACH SECTION; A WARM GREY META TONE SETS THEM APART FROM BLACK BODY INK.
- DECORATION IS STRUCTURAL, NOT ORNAMENTAL: NO DROP-SHADOW SOUP, NO GLASSMORPHISM, NO GRADIENT MESH. SHADOWS ARE WHISPER-SOFT AND USED SPARINGLY (NAV BAR, CARD HOVER).
- INTERACTIONS REWARD SCROLL: TILES FADE-AND-RISE INTO VIEW; SERVICE CARDS STACK STICKILY ON TOP OF ONE ANOTHER AS YOU SCROLL; A CONTINUOUS LOGO/INSIGHT MARQUEE GLIDES SIDEWAYS BEHIND A SOFT EDGE MASK.

## MOTION / ANIMATION / INTERACTION SPEC

- SCROLL REVEAL: ELEMENTS TAGGED FOR ANIMATION START AT `OPACITY 0` + `TRANSLATEY(30PX)` AND EASE TO VISIBLE WITH A 1S `CUBIC-BEZIER(0.22, 1, 0.36, 1)` TRANSITION, TRIGGERED BY AN `INTERSECTIONOBSERVER` (THRESHOLD ~0.1, REVEAL ONCE).
- STICKY SERVICE STACK: EACH CARD IS `STICKY` AT `TOP: ~100PX` SO SUCCESSIVE CARDS SLIDE UP AND PIN OVER THE PREVIOUS ONE, CREATING A CARD-DECK STACKING EFFECT.
- INSIGHTS TICKER: A `@KEYFRAMES` MARQUEE TRANSLATING `-50%` OVER ~30S LINEAR INFINITE, WITH THE CARD ROW DUPLICATED FOR A SEAMLESS LOOP AND A LEFT/RIGHT MASK-IMAGE FADE.
- BUTTON FEEDBACK: PRIMARY/LIME PILLS SCALE UP SLIGHTLY ON HOVER AND DOWN ON ACTIVE; OUTLINE BUTTONS INVERT TO FILLED ON HOVER.
- MICRO-INTERACTIONS: STAT CARDS LIFT WITH A SOFT SHADOW ON HOVER; PORTFOLIO ARROW CHIPS ROTATE FROM 45° TO 0° ON HOVER; BLOG IMAGES SCALE TO ~1.1 OVER 700MS; SOCIAL ICONS SWAP TO LIME ON HOVER; NAV LINKS BRIGHTEN FROM GREY TO INK.
- SMOOTH ANCHOR SCROLLING VIA `SCROLL-BEHAVIOR: SMOOTH`.

## RESPONSIVE BEHAVIOR

- DESKTOP (≥1024PX): MULTI-COLUMN GRIDS, FULL TILE HEIGHTS, STICKY SERVICE STACK ACTIVE, NAV LINKS INLINE.
