Process

How we work

A disciplined, three-stage workflow that removes the guesswork and delivers a flawless finish every time.

Step 01

Initial assessment

We inspect paint depth, swirls and contaminants under directional light to map the exact restoration plan for your vehicle.

Step 02

Precision correction

Decontamination, multi-stage machine polishing and deep cleaning using premium, surface-safe products.

Step 03

Protect & inspect

Ceramic protection is applied and cured, then a rigorous final inspection ensures every panel meets our standard.
