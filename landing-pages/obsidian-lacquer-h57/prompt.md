THE NAMED DESIGN LANGUAGE IS **"OBSIDIAN LACQUER"** — A DARK, CINEMATIC, SHOWROOM-FLOOR AESTHETIC THAT FEELS LIKE A LATE-NIGHT PHOTOSHOOT INSIDE A BLACKED-OUT DETAILING BAY: WET-LOOK BLACK PAINT UNDER A SINGLE RAKING LIGHT, A SCALPEL OF VERMILION RED FOR ENERGY, AND PRISTINE EDITORIAL TYPE. - **SCROLL REVEAL**: EVERY MAJOR BLOCK STARTS AT `opacity:0; translateY(20–28px)` AND EASES TO VISIBLE VIA INTERSECTIONOBSERVER (`threshold ~0.12`), 0.7–0.9s EASE-OUT, WITH SMALL PER-CARD STAGGER.
