## MOTION & INTERACTION SPEC

- **ICON BELT**: SEAMLESS INFINITE LEFTWARD STEP EVERY 3S USING CLONED LEADING CHIPS AND A NO-TRANSITION SNAP-BACK.
- **SCROLL REVEAL**: ELEMENTS TAGGED FOR ANIMATION START AT `opacity:0; translateY(16px)` AND EASE TO VISIBLE VIA AN `IntersectionObserver` (THRESHOLD ~0.12, UNOBSERVE AFTER FIRST REVEAL), STAGGERED WHERE NATURAL.
- **HERO PARALLAX**: MOUSE-MOVE DRIVES A PERSPECTIVE TILT ON THE HERO MOCKUP; CLAMPED, SMOOTHED, AND DISABLED UNDER `prefers-reduced-motion`.
- **NODE INTERACTION**: CLICKING THE AI NODE RESETS ITS PROGRESS BAR TO 0% THEN ANIMATES TO 100%; HOVER SHOWS A FLOATING PILL TOOLTIP.
- **CODE CARD**: ON HOVER, MONO LINES RE-APPEAR ONE-BY-ONE (STAGGERED ~100MS) WITH A SMALL LEFT-SLIDE; A FLOWING DASH ANIMATES ALONG CONNECTOR PATHS.
- **BUTTONS**: PRESS SCALES TO `0.96`; A CLICK RIPPLE EXPANDS FROM THE POINTER. CTA HOVER REDUCES OPACITY SLIGHTLY.
- **COUNTER**: LAST DIGIT SLIDES UP, FADES, RE-RANDOMIZES, AND SETTLES EVERY FEW SECONDS.
- **FOOTER WORDMARK SPOTLIGHT**: PER-LETTER COLOR/OPACITY/SCALE DRIVEN BY CURSOR DISTANCE.
- ALL DECORATIVE MOTION RESPECTS `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce`.
