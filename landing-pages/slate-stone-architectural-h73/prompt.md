# SLATE & STONE — ARCHITECTURAL REAL-ESTATE ADVISORY LANDING PAGE

## AESTHETIC IDENTITY

BUILD A SINGLE-PAGE, MULTI-SECTION MARKETING LANDING PAGE FOR A FICTIONAL ULTRA-HIGH-NET-WORTH ARCHITECTURAL REAL-ESTATE ADVISORY CALLED **"SLATE & STONE"**, LED BY A FICTIONAL PRINCIPAL AGENT NAMED **"ELIAS MORAND"**. THE NAMED AESTHETIC IS **"GALLERY MINIMALISM"** — A QUIET, EDITORIAL, FINE-ART-AUCTION-HOUSE FEELING WHERE ARCHITECTURE IS TREATED LIKE A COLLECTIBLE OBJECT. THE WHOLE PAGE READS LIKE A PRINTED MONOGRAPH: HAIRLINE RULES, GENEROUS WHITE SPACE, OVERSIZED CONDENSED UPPERCASE DISPLAY TYPE, AND A WARM PAPER GROUND RATHER THAN PURE WHITE. NO GENERIC SAAS GRADIENTS, NO ROUNDED CARDS WITH DROP SHADOWS, NO STOCK GLASSMORPHISM. THE MOOD IS RESTRAINED, CONFIDENT, AND EXPENSIVE.

## COLOR SYSTEM

- INK (PRIMARY TEXT / DARK PANELS): `#16191A` (NEAR-BLACK CHARCOAL).
- PAPER (PAGE GROUND): `#F4F1EA` (WARM OFF-WHITE LINEN).
- PAPER-LIGHT (CARDS / INSET PANELS): `#FBF9F3`.
- STONE (MUTED MIDTONE FOR HAIRLINES / META TEXT): `RGBA(22,25,26,0.55)`.
- HAIRLINE RULES: `RGBA(22,25,26,0.18)`.
- ACCENT — "OXIDE" (A SINGLE SPARING WARM RUST/TERRACOTTA): `#B4522E`, USED ONLY FOR THE STATUS DOT, SECTION INDEX NUMERALS, ACTIVE STATES, AND ONE WORD OF EMPHASIS — NEVER FOR LARGE FILLS.
- ON-INK CREAM (TEXT ON DARK PANELS): `#F4F1EA`.

## TYPOGRAPHY

- DISPLAY / HEADINGS: A TIGHT, CONDENSED-ISH GROTESQUE — USE **"FAMILJEN GROTESK"** (WEIGHTS 400/500/600/700). ALL MAJOR HEADINGS ARE UPPERCASE WITH NEGATIVE TRACKING (`-0.04EM` TO `-0.07EM`) AND LINE-HEIGHT NEAR `0.92`–`0.98`. HERO H1 RANGES `CLAMP(2.8REM, 8VW, 6REM)`.
- BODY / META / UI LABELS: A NEUTRAL SANS — **"INTER"** (OR ARIA/HELVETICA FALLBACK), USED FOR PARAGRAPHS, LABELS, AND ALL SMALL UPPERCASE EYEBROWS WITH WIDE LETTER-SPACING (`0.25EM`–`0.4EM`).
- VENDOR BOTH FONT FAMILIES LOCALLY (SELF-HOSTED WOFF2 + `@FONT-FACE`); DO NOT HOTLINK GOOGLE FONTS AT RUNTIME.

## MOTION / ANIMATION / INTERACTION SPEC

- **SCROLL REVEALS**: USE AN `INTERSECTIONOBSERVER` SO MARKED ELEMENTS START AT `OPACITY:0; TRANSLATEY(24PX)` AND EASE TO `OPACITY:1; TRANSLATEY(0)` OVER `~0.8S` WITH A SOFT EASE-OUT, STAGGERED PER SECTION. OBSERVE-ONCE (UNOBSERVE AFTER REVEAL).
- **HERO INTRO**: ON LOAD, THE EYEBROW, H1 (IDEALLY LINE-BY-LINE OR WORD-BY-WORD), LEAD, AND CTAS RESOLVE IN WITH A SHORT STAGGERED BLUR/FADE/RISE; THE HERO IMAGE PERFORMS A SUBTLE SCALE-DOWN-TO-REST OR CLIP REVEAL.
- **IMAGE HOVERS**: PROPERTY AND HERO IMAGES SCALE `~1.05`–`1.10` OVER `~700MS` ON HOVER WITHIN A FIXED OVERFLOW-HIDDEN FRAME.
- **BUTTONS / LINKS**: SMOOTH `300MS` TRANSITIONS; OUTLINE CTA INVERTS TO INK FILL + CREAM TEXT ON HOVER; UNDERLINED "VIEW DOSSIER" LINKS SHIFT.
- **STATS COUNT-UP** (OPTIONAL POLISH): NUMERALS COUNT UP WHEN THE STATS STRIP ENTERS VIEW.
- **HEADER**: SUBTLE STATE CHANGE (SLIGHTLY STRONGER BLUR / SHADOWLESS RULE) AFTER SCROLLING PAST THE HERO.
- **MOBILE MENU**: FULL-SCREEN PAPER OVERLAY THAT FADES/SLIDES IN WITH OVERSIZED STAGGERED LINKS; LOCK BODY SCROLL WHILE OPEN.
- RESPECT `PREFERS-REDUCED-MOTION` BY DISABLING TRANSFORMS/TRANSITIONS.
- A CUSTOM THIN (`4PX`) SCROLLBAR THEMED TO PAPER TRACK / INK THUMB.

## RESPONSIVE BEHAVIOR

- DESKTOP (`≥MD`): ASYMMETRIC SPLITS, VERTICAL HAIRLINE DIVIDERS, THREE-UP PROPERTY GRID, FOUR-UP STATS, 2×2 SERVICES.
- TABLET: STATS/SERVICES MAY DROP TO TWO COLUMNS; PROPERTY GRID MAY STACK.
- MOBILE (`<MD`): ALL SPLITS STACK TO A SINGLE COLUMN (TEXT THEN IMAGE), VERTICAL DIVIDERS HIDE AND BECOME HORIZONTAL HAIRLINE RULES, NAV COLLAPSES TO HAMBURGER, DISPLAY TYPE SCALES DOWN VIA `CLAMP`, PADDING TIGHTENS TO `~24PX`. NO HORIZONTAL OVERFLOW AT ANY WIDTH.
