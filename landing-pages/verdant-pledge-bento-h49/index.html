Have Questions?

Answers to the things people ask us most about getting involved.

How can I volunteer?
Tap "Get Involved" in the navigation, fill out our short steward application, and a regional coordinator will reach out within 48 hours with the missions closest to you.
Where do donations go?
100% of public donations fund on-the-ground restoration. Our operating costs are covered separately through private grants and corporate partnerships.
Can I start a local chapter?
Absolutely. We provide a starter kit, training, and mentorship for anyone passionate about bringing Verdant Pledge to their own community.
