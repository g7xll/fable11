# CARTWRIGHT & CO — WHITE-GLOVE RELOCATION LANDING PAGE

## AESTHETIC IDENTITY

BUILD A COMPLETE, MULTI-SECTION MARKETING LANDING PAGE FOR A PREMIUM, CRAFT-LED RELOCATION & LOGISTICS COMPANY CALLED **CARTWRIGHT & CO**. THE NAMED AESTHETIC IS **"WARM ARTISAN LOGISTICS"** — A SOFT, CONFIDENT, EDITORIAL TAKE ON A MOVING BRAND THAT FEELS LIKE A BOUTIQUE HOSPITALITY COMPANY RATHER THAN A FREIGHT WAREHOUSE. THE MOOD IS REASSURING, TACTILE, AND HUMAN: TERRACOTTA WARMTH, CREAM PAPER, GENEROUS WHITESPACE, ROUNDED GEOMETRY, AND ONE SHARP SERIF ACCENT WORD PER HEADLINE. EVERYTHING SHOULD READ AS "YOUR BELONGINGS ARE IN CAREFUL HANDS."

## COLOR SYSTEM

- PRIMARY BRAND / ACCENT — TERRACOTTA CLAY `#D9603B` (USED FOR CTAS, SERIF ACCENT WORDS, ICONS, COUNTERS, HOVER FILLS).
- INK / NEAR-BLACK — `#211C18` (HEADINGS, DARK SECTIONS, BODY TEXT).
- CREAM PAPER BACKGROUND — `#FAF4EC` (PAGE BASE).
- WARM OFF-WHITE PANEL — `#FFFDFA` (CARDS, FORM PANELS).
- SAND / BISCUIT — `#EFE3D2` (SOFT CARD FILLS, SECTION TINTS).
- SUPPORTING TINTS — DUSTY SAGE `#E5EBE2`, MUTED SKY `#E4ECF2`, PALE BLUSH `#F6E4DC` (FOR MEGA-MENU / SERVICE CARD HOVER STATES).
- MUTED TEXT — WARM GREY `#6F665C`.

## TYPOGRAPHY

- PRIMARY UI / BODY — **INTER** (400, 500, 600, 700).
- DISPLAY / HEADINGS — **DM SANS** (500, 700), TIGHT TRACKING.
- SERIF ACCENT — **FRAUNCES** (OR PLAYFAIR DISPLAY) ITALIC, 500–600, USED ONLY FOR ONE EMPHASIS WORD PER HEADLINE IN TERRACOTTA.
- HEADLINES ARE LARGE (CLAMPED 3REM–5REM), MEDIUM WEIGHT, NEGATIVE LETTER-SPACING. EYEBROW LABELS ARE 10PX UPPERCASE, BOLD, WIDE TRACKING, INSIDE A SOFT TERRACOTTA PILL.

## MOTION, ANIMATION & INTERACTION SPEC

- SCROLL-REVEAL: ELEMENTS WITH A DATA ATTRIBUTE FADE + TRANSLATE IN (FROM LEFT OR FROM BOTTOM) VIA INTERSECTIONOBSERVER WITH A SOFT CUBIC-BEZIER EASE.
- HERO IMAGE TICKERS: TWO CSS KEYFRAME LOOPS (UP / DOWN) TRANSLATING -50% OVER ~22S, CONTENT DUPLICATED FOR SEAMLESS LOOPING.
- PARTNER MARQUEE: HORIZONTAL `translateX(-50%)` LOOP, PAUSE ON HOVER, EDGE MASK FADE.
- COUNTERS: REQUESTANIMATIONFRAME EASE-OUT COUNT-UP TRIGGERED ONCE ON INTERSECTION.
- PROCESS TIMELINE: LINE WIDTH ANIMATES TO 100% AND CIRCLES ACTIVATE WITH A STAGGERED `setTimeout` ON INTERSECTION.
- TESTIMONIAL DECK: AUTO-ADVANCE INTERVAL + CLICK HANDLER; TOP CARD TRANSLATES/ROTATES OUT, STACK RE-LAYERS WITH SCALE/TRANSLATE.
- BUTTON HOVER: A TERRACOTTA PANEL `scale-x(0)→scale-x(1)` WIPES IN FROM THE LEFT BEHIND THE LABEL; ARROW ICONS NUDGE RIGHT.
- NAVBAR SHRINKS / GAINS OPACITY ON SCROLL; MOBILE MENU SLIDES IN FROM THE RIGHT.
- RESPECT REDUCED-MOTION WHERE REASONABLE.
