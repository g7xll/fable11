## MOTION / ANIMATION / INTERACTION SPEC

- **SCROLL-REVEAL:** EACH MAJOR SECTION FADES UP (`opacity 0 → 1`, `translateY(20px) → 0`) OVER ~0.8S EASE-OUT WHEN IT ENTERS THE VIEWPORT, DRIVEN BY AN `IntersectionObserver` (THRESHOLD ~0.1) THAT UNOBSERVES AFTER REVEAL.
- **IMAGE HOVER:** PHOTOS DESATURATE BY DEFAULT (`grayscale(0.2)`) AND RETURN TO FULL COLOR OVER ~1S ON HOVER.
- **CTA HOVER:** THE GREEN "VIEW MENU" BAR BRIGHTENS SLIGHTLY AND ITS ARROW GLYPH TRANSLATES UP-RIGHT; LINKS UNDERLINE/COLOR-SHIFT TO GREEN.
- **MOBILE MENU:** SLIDE-IN FROM RIGHT OVER ~0.5S EASE-IN-OUT, CLOSING ON LINK-TAP OR CLOSE BUTTON.
- ALL MOTION IS SUBTLE AND CALM — NO BOUNCY SPRINGS, NO PARALLAX CHAOS. RESPECT `prefers-reduced-motion` BY SHOWING CONTENT IMMEDIATELY.
