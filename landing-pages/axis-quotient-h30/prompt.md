BUILD A FULLY RESPONSIVE, MULTI-SECTION MARKETING LANDING PAGE FOR A FICTIONAL QUANTITATIVE STRATEGY CONSULTANCY NAMED **AXIS QUOTIENT** — "DECISIONS, REDUCED TO EVIDENCE." THE OPPOSITE OF SOFT, ROUNDED SAAS. EVERYTHING SITS ON A TIGHT EDITORIAL GRID, ANCHORED BY NEAR-BLACK INK, SET ON WARM IVORY PAPER, AND PUNCTURED BY A SINGLE ELECTRIC COBALT ACCENT. NO PURPLE GRADIENTS, NO GLASSMORPHISM, NO DECORATIVE BLUR. CONFIDENT, MEASURED, ENGINEERED.

## DESIGN LANGUAGE & MOOD

- MOOD: PRECISION, RESTRAINT, AUTHORITY. A FIRM THAT SELLS CERTAINTY. THE PAGE SHOULD READ LIKE A PRINTED ANNUAL REPORT THAT HAPPENED TO BECOME INTERACTIVE.
- TACTILE EDITORIAL GRID: HAIRLINE RULES, SECTION INDEX NUMBERS `(01)`–`(07)`, MONOSPACE META LABELS, AND GENEROUS NEGATIVE SPACE.
- HARD GEOMETRY: SHARP OR NEAR-SHARP CORNERS (2–4PX MAX), 1PX BORDERS, NO DROP SHADOWS EXCEPT A SINGLE LIFTED HERO CARD.
- ONE OVERSIZED DISPLAY WORDMARK PER FULL-BLEED MOMENT (`QUOTIENT`, `MANDATE`) AT 12–18VW.
