# ELECTRIC VOLT FOLIO — A "KINETIC BRUTALIST" MOTION DESIGNER PORTFOLIO

BUILD A FULLY RESPONSIVE, MULTI-SECTION PERSONAL PORTFOLIO LANDING PAGE FOR A FICTIONAL MOTION DESIGNER NAMED **NOVA REYES** — "I DON'T MOVE PIXELS, I MOVE EMOTIONS." THE NAMED DESIGN LANGUAGE IS **"KINETIC BRUTALISM"**: A LOUD, CONFIDENT, UNAPOLOGETICALLY TYPOGRAPHIC NEO-BRUTALIST POSTER AESTHETIC THAT FEELS LIKE A RISOGRAPH ZINE CROSSED WITH A 3D MOTION REEL. EVERYTHING IS HARD-EDGED, HIGH-CONTRAST, GRID-DRIVEN, AND IN-YOUR-FACE — BLACK INK ON ACID-GREEN PAPER, PUNCHED THROUGH WITH HOT-PINK ACCENTS, HEAVY BORDERS, AND BLOCKY DROP-SHADOWS. THE OPPOSITE OF SOFT, ROUNDED, GLASSY SAAS. IT SHOULD FEEL EXPENSIVE, REBELLIOUS, AND ALIVE WITH MOTION.

## DESIGN LANGUAGE & MOOD

- NEO-BRUTALIST POSTER ART: THICK 3–4PX BLACK BORDERS ON EVERYTHING, HARD BLOCKY OFFSET DROP-SHADOWS (4PX/8PX SOLID BLACK, NO BLUR), NO ROUNDED CORNERS EXCEPT DELIBERATE PILL BUTTONS.
- HIGH-CONTRAST, MAXIMALIST, SLIGHTLY CHAOTIC BUT PRECISELY GRIDDED. PANELS ROTATE A FEW DEGREES AND STRAIGHTEN ON HOVER.
- VISIBLE STRUCTURE: A FAINT 100PX BLACK GRID OVERLAY ON ACID-GREEN SECTIONS; A FINER 40PX TRANSLUCENT GRID ON DARK SECTIONS.
- MOOD: BOLD, KINETIC, A LITTLE PUNK, CONFIDENT, PROFESSIONAL. EVERY FRAME FEELS DELIBERATE.
