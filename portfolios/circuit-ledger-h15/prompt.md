# CIRCUIT LEDGER — A DEVELOPER PORTFOLIO IN "QUIET CIRCUITRY" AESTHETIC

## AESTHETIC IDENTITY

BUILD A SINGLE-PAGE, FORCED-DARK DEVELOPER PORTFOLIO NAMED **CIRCUIT LEDGER** IN A DESIGN LANGUAGE CALLED **"QUIET CIRCUITRY."** THE MOOD IS A NEAR-BLACK ENGINEERING WORKBENCH AT NIGHT: A FULL-VIEWPORT LIVE CANVAS WHERE FAINT LIGHT-RUNNERS CRAWL ALONG A GRID LIKE CURRENT THROUGH A CIRCUIT BOARD, OVERLAID WITH A REFINED EDITORIAL TYPESYSTEM. THE PERSONALITY IS PART HACKER-TERMINAL, PART HIGH-END PRINT — DISCIPLINED, WARM, AND CONFIDENT, NEVER NEON OR GAMER-RGB. CHAMPAGNE-CREAM IS THE SIGNATURE ACCENT; A SOFT CIRCUIT-GREEN IS THE SECONDARY; EVERYTHING ELSE IS GRAYSCALE ON A VOID BACKGROUND.

## GLOBAL BACKGROUND — THE LIVE CIRCUIT CANVAS

A FIXED FULL-VIEWPORT `<canvas>` BEHIND ALL CONTENT (Z-0), CONTENT WRAPPED ABOVE AT Z-10. RENDER EACH FRAME WITH A SEMI-TRANSPARENT VOID FILL (`rgba(5,5,5,0.15)`) TO PRODUCE FADING TRAILS. DRAW:
1. A FAINT STATIC GRID (50PX CELLS, `rgba(255,255,255,0.015)` STROKE).
2. ~40 **CIRCUIT RUNNERS**: PARTICLES THAT TRAVEL STRICTLY ALONG GRID LINES, TURNING 90° ONLY AT INTERSECTIONS (≈15% CHANCE), LEAVING A GLOWING TRAIL OF 10–30 SEGMENTS, EACH IN ONE OF FOUR ACCENT TINTS (CHAMPAGNE, GREEN, ICE-BLUE, PINK) AT LOW ALPHA WITH A SMALL SHADOW-BLUR, PLUS A BRIGHT 3PX "HEAD." RUNNERS HAVE A FINITE LIFE AND RESPAWN AT RANDOM SNAPPED GRID POINTS.
3. ~80 FAINT MONOSPACE `0`/`1` GLYPHS SCATTERED ON GRID CELLS THAT OCCASIONALLY FLIP AND RELOCATE.
4. A MOUSE-FOLLOWING HIGHLIGHTED GRID CELL (CHAMPAGNE STROKE + GLOW) THAT SNAPS TO THE CELL UNDER THE CURSOR.
A BOTTOM-FADE GRADIENT OVERLAY MELTS THE CANVAS INTO THE VOID. THE CANVAS RESIZES AND RE-INITS ON WINDOW RESIZE. RESPECT `prefers-reduced-motion` (FREEZE/STILL FALLBACK).

## MOTION & INTERACTION SPEC

- SCROLL-REVEAL: ELEMENTS WITH A `reveal` CLASS START AT OPACITY 0 / TRANSLATEY 30PX AND TRANSITION TO VISIBLE OVER ~1S VIA AN INTERSECTIONOBSERVER (THRESHOLD ~0.1), UNOBSERVING AFTER REVEAL.
- HOVER: CARDS BRIGHTEN AND TINT THEIR BORDER; PORTRAIT STRAIGHTENS + COLORIZES; CHIPS TINT; BUTTONS SCALE 1.02–1.05.
- TYPEWRITER STACK + ANIMATED PROFICIENCY BARS BOTH TRIGGER ON FIRST INTERSECTION, ONCE.
- CANVAS RUNS AT REQUESTANIMATIONFRAME; MOUSE HIGHLIGHT FOLLOWS CURSOR.
- SMOOTH-SCROLL ANCHOR NAV; SCROLL-SPY ACTIVE LINK; CUSTOM CHAMPAGNE-HOVER SCROLLBAR.
- ALL ANIMATIONS GATE BEHIND `prefers-reduced-motion`.
