How It's Made

Four steps, no shortcuts.

01

We farm one block at a time, picking by taste rather than by date. Yields stay low on purpose.

02

Whole clusters go in cool. Native yeast starts the ferment. We add nothing and wait.

03

The wine rests in old oak below ground, racked by gravity, tasted weekly until it settles.

04

Bottled unfined, unfiltered, labeled by hand. Each case carries the lot and the date.
