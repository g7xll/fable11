About Scalar

Built for developers,
backed by community.

Scalar started as an open-source side project and grew into a CMS trusted by thousands of teams. We build in the open, ship fast, and stay close to the developers who use us every day.

Our mission

We believe content infrastructure should feel like great developer tooling — fast, typed, and version-controlled. Scalar gives teams a content layer they can model in code, query through clean APIs, and ship without fighting their stack.

Our team

A small, distributed team of engineers and designers spread across eight time zones. We're former platform, infra, and DX builders who got tired of brittle CMS tools and decided to build the one we wanted.
