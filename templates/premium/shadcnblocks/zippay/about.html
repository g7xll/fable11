Our Mission

Our Mission

While we absolutely love productivity software, we believe enhancing workplace efficiency is, in general, a broken thing. There's just too many tools to keep track of, too many things is entirely separate ecosystems. There has to be a better way to work — that's why we created Zippay. You've internal tasks now an a way to fulfill our vision of making the world a more productive place.

Eventually, our goal is to move all work into a single platform — thereby making people more productive and giving them at least 20% of time to dedicate to other things. One way to express them all. We're just getting started, and we're grateful for all the 2 million teams that are in this together with our team.
