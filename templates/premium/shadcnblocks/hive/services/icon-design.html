Marks that scale

Icon Design

Icons are the small marks that carry big meaning. We design custom icon sets and supporting symbols that stay clear at any size — from a tiny app badge to a large signage mark. Built on a consistent grid with shared visual language, our icons strengthen recognition, guide users intuitively, and give your brand the flexibility to work everywhere it appears.

What you get

Custom icon set

A cohesive family of icons drawn on a shared grid and stroke system.

App symbols

Pixel-perfect marks and badges optimized for app stores and devices.

Usage guidelines

Clear rules for sizing, spacing, and color so icons stay consistent.

01

Audit & Scope

03
