The challenge

Nextdock needed to stand out in a crowded logistics market while signalling speed, reliability, and a startup's appetite for change — without looking like every other tech logo.

The solution

We built a complete identity system around a sharp, motion-led mark, paired with a confident type scale and a flexible palette that scales from app icon to billboard.

The result

The new brand helped Nextdock win early adopters and close their first funding round, giving them a memorable identity to grow into.
