Our story

Hive was built on a simple idea: every brand deserves a logo that people remember. Too many businesses settle for visuals that feel generic or forgettable. We wanted to change that.

What started as a small design studio has grown into a dedicated agency focused entirely on logo and identity design. From our first projects with local startups to collaborations with established brands, our goal has stayed the same — to create logos that are simple, timeless, and full of meaning.

Over the years, we've learned that a logo is more than a mark. It's a signal of trust, a first impression, and often the most visible part of a business. That's why we combine strategy and creativity in everything we do — listening closely, researching deeply, and designing with care.
