Our story

Hive was built on a simple idea: every brand deserves a logo that people remember. Too many businesses settle for visuals that feel generic or forgettable. We wanted to change that.

What started as a small design studio has grown into a dedicated agency focused entirely on logo and identity design. From our first projects with local startups to collaborations with established brands, our goal has stayed the same — to create logos that are simple, timeless, and full of meaning.

Over the years, we've learned that a logo is more than a mark. It's a signal of trust, a first impression, and often the most visible part of a business. That's why we combine strategy and creativity in everything we do — listening closely, researching deeply, and designing with care.

Identities that last
Brand Identity

Your brand identity is more than a logo — it's the complete visual language that represents who you are. From typography and color palettes to photography style and design elements, a cohesive brand identity builds recognition, communicates professionalism, and creates emotional connections with your audience. It's the foundation that makes your brand memorable across every touchpoint.

What you get
Logo system

Primary, secondary, and alternate logo variations for different uses.

Color palette

A complete color system with primary, secondary, and accent colors.

Typography guide

Carefully selected fonts and type hierarchy for all your communications.

Brand Identity process
Brand Discovery

We dive deep into your brand values, target audience, and competitive landscape to understand what makes you unique.

Strategy & Direction

We define your brand positioning, personality, and visual direction through mood boards and strategic frameworks.

Identity Design

We create your complete visual system — logo, colors, typography, patterns, and all supporting elements.

Guidelines Creation

We document everything in a comprehensive brand guide that ensures consistent application across all media.

Asset Delivery

You receive all logo files, brand assets, templates, and guidelines ready to implement across your business.

Charter
Project overview

Working closely with a mission-driven company from their earliest stages, we created an identity that reflects their values of sustainability and innovation. The new brand helped them establish credibility.

Client

Charter

Date

January 2024

Industry

Legal Services

Services

Brand Identity

Charter process
The challenge

Charter was reimagining legal services for startups but their existing brand felt too traditional and intimidating. They needed an identity that would make legal expertise feel accessible and aligned with the entrepreneurial spirit of their clients.

Our solution

We designed a brand that balances professionalism with approachability. The mark combines classical legal symbolism with modern geometric forms, while the color palette and typography create a fresh, confident presence that stands apart from traditional law firm branding.

The result

Charter's rebrand dramatically shifted perception among their target audience. Client acquisition increased by 60% in the following year, and the brand became recognized as a thought leader in the startup legal space through consistent, distinctive visual presence.
