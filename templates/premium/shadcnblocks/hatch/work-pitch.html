Pitch

View live

Visual identity and product pages for a football boot built for firm ground and media moments—bold, pitch-ready, and unmistakable on a wall of sameness.

Role
Freelance Brand Designer
Timeline
5 weeks
Focus
Football, E-com, Retail

The Challenge

Pitch was entering a crowded boot market with a genuinely strong stud pattern—but visually they looked like a generic team supplier. They needed an identity that reads pitch-ready: loud enough for young players, credible enough for coaches, and flexible enough for limited drops and club partnerships.

What I Did

I leaned into the name: pitch as in the field, pitch as in the story you sell to scouts and sponsors. The identity uses high-contrast typography, pitch-grid patterns, and photography direction that shows grip and turn—not just static product floats. I designed PDP modules for stud layout and fit so buyers aren't guessing in the cart, and gave them poster art that works in shops and Instagram alike.

Deliverables

Brand Story (Field + Media Kit Narrative)
Logo + Pattern System For Boot + Box
PDP Template (Specs, Stud Map, Fit Guide)
Retailer Deck + Locker Room Poster Art
Social Teaser + Reveal Campaign Layouts
Color + Material Naming For Two Colorways
