Veil

View live

Packaging and site design for a minimalist skincare line—clinical calm meets soft warmth, with a freelancer-led system from jar to checkout.

Role
Freelance Brand & Web Designer
Timeline
4 weeks
Focus
Beauty, D2C, Packaging

The Challenge

Veil had formulas they loved and lab partners lined up, but the brand felt like “another beige skincare drop.” They needed a story about clarity and gentle coverage—without clinical coldness or faux wellness poetry.

What I Did

I defined Veil as light structure for skin: simple steps, honest ingredients, no fake science graphs. I designed a packaging system with generous whitespace, one serif for warmth, one sans for clarity, and color coding per step so the shelf reads calm and intentional.
