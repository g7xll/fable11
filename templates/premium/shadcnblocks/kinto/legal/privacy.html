Privacy Policy

Information we collect

When you use Kinto, we collect information you provide directly — such as your name, email address, and journal entries. We also collect basic usage data to improve the app experience, including device type, app version, and general interaction patterns.

Your journal entries are your most personal data. We treat them with the highest level of care and encryption.

How we use your information

We use your information to provide and improve the Kinto journaling experience. This includes syncing your entries across devices, delivering personalized prompts, and sending optional reminders.

We never sell your personal data to third parties. We never use your journal content for advertising. We never train AI models on your private entries.

Data storage and security

All journal entries are encrypted end-to-end. This means only you can read your entries — not even our team has access to your content.

Your data is stored on secure, SOC 2 compliant servers. We use industry-standard encryption protocols for data in transit and at rest. Regular security audits ensure our infrastructure meets the highest standards.

Your rights

You have the right to access, export, or delete your data at any time. You can download a complete copy of your journal entries from the app settings. If you choose to delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed within 30 days.

For users in the European Economic Area, you have additional rights under the GDPR, including the right to data portability and the right to restrict processing.

Cookies and tracking

Kinto's website uses essential cookies to maintain your session and preferences. We use privacy-focused analytics to understand how visitors interact with our site — no personal data is shared with advertising networks.

You can disable non-essential cookies at any time through your browser settings.

Third-party services

We use a limited number of third-party services to operate Kinto, including cloud hosting, email delivery, and payment processing. Each provider is vetted for their privacy and security practices and is contractually bound to protect your data.

Children's privacy

Kinto is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can remove it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we'll notify you through the app or via email. Continued use of Kinto after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, reach out to us at privacy@kinto.app.
