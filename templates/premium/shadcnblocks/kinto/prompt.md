## SUMMARY

KINTO IS A WARM, EDITORIAL MARKETING SITE FOR A PRIVACY-FIRST JOURNALING APP ("A JOURNAL THAT GROWS WITH YOU"). THE LOOK IS CREAM/PAPER LIGHT MODE WITH A NEAR-BLACK DARK MODE, GOLD/TAN ACCENT, LARGE DM SERIF DISPLAY HEADINGS WITH ITALIC ACCENT WORDS, DM SANS BODY, AND JETBRAINS MONO FOR SMALL UPPERCASE LABELS / NUMBERED EYEBROWS. SECTIONS REVEAL ON SCROLL (FADE + RISE). A THEME TOGGLE IN THE HEADER SWITCHES LIGHT/DARK AND PERSISTS.
