Your questions answered.

Everything you need to know about Kinto

Still have questions? Reach out anytime.

01

What makes Kinto different from other journaling apps?

We built Kinto for ourselves first. There's no investor pressure to bolt on features or harvest your data — just a calm, focused writing space with AI reflections that read between the lines. Most apps optimize for engagement; we optimize for the quiet clarity of a good journaling habit.

02

How does a two-person team handle privacy?

Privacy isn't a feature we delegate — it's the foundation we built on. Every entry is end-to-end encrypted before it leaves your device, so we couldn't read your journal even if we wanted to. A small team means fewer hands on your data and a single, accountable standard for how it's protected.

03

You're bootstrapped — is Kinto sustainable?

Yes. We're profitable and growing on subscription revenue alone, with zero outside funding. That means our only incentive is to keep you happy enough to keep writing. No runway to burn, no pressure to exit — Kinto is built to be here for the long haul.

04

Can I export my data?

Always. Your full journal is yours to take at any time — export everything as PDF, markdown, or JSON in a couple of clicks. And if you ever choose to leave, deleting your account erases your data permanently, with no copies kept on our end.

05

Are you hiring?

We're carefully growing from two founders to four. Right now we're looking for a Product Engineer and a Growth Designer who want to work directly with founders and ship fast. If that sounds like you, reach out — we read every application ourselves.
