Pricing
Our pricing starts at $19/month for the Starter plan, which includes 3 AI agents and 1,000 task executions. The Pro plan at $79/month gives you unlimited agents and 25,000 executions. Enterprise pricing is custom-quoted based on your scale and requirements.
Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial on all plans with no credit card required. You can explore all features including autonomous workflows, agent deployment, and our full SDK integration.
We'll notify you when you're approaching your limits. You can choose to upgrade your plan, purchase additional execution credits, or we'll pause new task queuing until the next billing cycle — your agents won't be terminated mid-task.
Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately with prorated billing. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for new subscriptions. If you're not satisfied, contact our support team and we'll process a full refund with no questions asked.
Agents
Our AI agents can automate virtually any repetitive or rule-based task: data processing, content generation, customer support routing, code review, report generation, API integrations, scheduling, monitoring, and complex multi-step workflows that involve decision-making.
Deployment is done through our dashboard UI or via our SDK (JavaScript, Python, Go, and Rust are supported). You can define agent behaviors, set triggers (time-based, event-based, or API-triggered), chain agents together, and monitor their execution in real time.
Yes, with configurable autonomy levels. You can set agents to require human approval for high-stakes decisions, operate fully autonomously within defined guardrails, or anything in between. All decisions are logged and auditable.
Legal
Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We act as a data processor under your instructions, never use your data to train our models, and never sell it to third parties. You retain full ownership of all data your agents process.
Yes. We are fully GDPR compliant and operate under a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) available on request. We are also CCPA compliant and offer data deletion, export, and opt-out mechanisms for all end users.
