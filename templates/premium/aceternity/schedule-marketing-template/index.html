FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Shape.AI and how does it work?
Shape.AI is a smart call scheduling platform that lets you book, manage, and automate calls with a single click. Connect your calendar, share your link, and let AI handle the rest — from scheduling to summaries.
Which calendar apps does Shape.AI integrate with?
Shape.AI integrates with Google Calendar, Microsoft Outlook, Apple Calendar, and any CalDAV-compatible calendar. All your events stay in sync automatically across platforms.
Can I record and transcribe my calls?
Yes! Shape.AI records all your calls with crystal-clear audio and generates AI-powered transcripts and meeting summaries automatically. Available on the Good and Enterprise plans.
Is there a free trial available?
Absolutely! Our Hobby plan is completely free forever with 5 scheduled calls per month. You can upgrade to a paid plan anytime to unlock unlimited calls and AI features.
How secure is my data on Shape.AI?
Your data security is our top priority. All call recordings and data are encrypted end-to-end using AES-256 encryption. We are SOC 2 Type II compliant and GDPR-ready.
