Rituals Over Choreography

Remote work succeeds when teams adopt reliable rituals: weekly syncs, async updates, and clear onboarding. Rituals reduce the need for perfect communication.

Keep Things Async-First

Favor documented decisions and written context. Use short standups for alignment, not status updates.

Over-Communicate Norms

Make expectations explicit: response times, meeting cadence, and code review SLAs. Clarity reduces friction.

Wrap Up

Trust, autonomy, and predictable processes are the pillars of remote success. Invest time in tooling and rituals early.
