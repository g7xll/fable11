Frequently asked questions

What is Proactic?
Proactic is an AI-powered marketing automation platform that helps businesses automate their social media, email marketing, and lead generation efforts.
How does Proactic work?
Proactic uses AI to analyze your audience and create personalized marketing campaigns across multiple channels automatically.
Which social media platforms does Proactic support?
Proactic supports Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, and more social media platforms.
Can I schedule posts in advance with Proactic?
Yes, you can schedule posts weeks or months in advance with our intelligent calendar system.
Does Proactic provide analytics?
Yes, we provide detailed analytics including engagement rates, click-through rates, and audience insights.
Is Proactic suitable for small businesses?
Absolutely! We have plans starting from free that are perfect for small businesses and solo entrepreneurs.
Can I collaborate with my team on Proactic?
Yes, Proactic supports team collaboration with role-based access and shared workspaces.
Does Proactic offer customer support?
We offer 24/7 customer support via chat and email, with dedicated account managers for Pro+ plans.
Is there a free trial available for Proactic?
Yes, our Hobby plan is completely free with no credit card required.
How can I get started with Proactic?
Simply click 'Get Started' or 'Book a demo' above to create your account and start automating.
