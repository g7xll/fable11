Arimo / Marketing

Digital Transformation Strategy

We partnered with Arimo to completely overhaul their digital presence and marketing approach. The goal was to modernize their brand communication and build a data-driven strategy that would generate qualified leads at scale.

Client

Arimo Inc.

Category

Digital Marketing

Year

2024

Duration

6 Months

The Challenge

Arimo had been relying on outdated marketing channels and lacked a cohesive digital strategy. Their website traffic was stagnant, conversion rates were low, and there was little alignment between their marketing and sales teams. They needed a full digital transformation — not just a new campaign, but a fundamental shift in how they approached growth.

Our Approach

We began with a deep-dive audit of their existing digital footprint — website performance, content strategy, paid channels, CRM data, and competitor landscape. From there, we developed a phased transformation roadmap covering:

  • SEO overhaul and content strategy realignment
  • Paid media restructuring across Google and LinkedIn
  • Marketing automation and lead nurturing workflows
  • Sales and marketing alignment workshops
  • Real-time analytics dashboards for ongoing optimisation

Results

Within six months of implementation, Arimo saw measurable improvements across every key metric. The transformation delivered results that exceeded initial projections and set a new benchmark for their growth trajectory.

Conclusion

This project reinforced that true digital transformation isn't about deploying new tools — it's about changing how an organisation thinks about growth. By aligning strategy, data, and execution, Arimo is now positioned as a market leader in their space with a scalable, repeatable growth engine.
