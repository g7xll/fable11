## SUMMARY

AISTARTERKIT IS A NEXT.JS-POWERED MARKETING SITE FOR A SELF-HOSTED AI STARTER KIT PRODUCT (TEXT/IMAGE/CODE/VIDEO/EMAIL GENERATORS). THE PUBLIC, LOGIN-FREE SURFACE IS A HOME LANDING PAGE, A CONTACT FORM, A PRICING PAGE WITH FAQ, A PRIVACY POLICY PAGE, AND SIGN-IN/SIGN-UP/RESET-PASSWORD AUTH-FORM PAGES. TWO ROUTES (`/text-generator`, `/email-generator`) REDIRECT TO `/signin` (HTTP 307) — THESE ARE GATED APP-DASHBOARD PAGES AND ARE EXCLUDED FROM THE CLONE; ONLY THE OPEN, STATIC-UI PAGES ARE REPRODUCED. THE SITE SUPPORTS LIGHT AND DARK MODE VIA A TAILWIND `dark` CLASS STRATEGY TOGGLED BY A MOON/SUN ICON BUTTON IN THE HEADER.

  - PAGE BACKGROUND: `bg-gray-50` (≈#F9FAFB) FOR BODY; WHITE (`#FFFFFF`) HEADER AND CARD SURFACES; SOFT RADIAL PURPLE/PINK/BLUE GLOW BLOBS BEHIND HERO AND AUTH-CARD SECTIONS.
  - DARK FOOTER/CTA BAND: NEAR-BLACK NAVY (`#101828` / `#0B1120`-ISH), USED FOR THE "KEY BENEFITS" DARK SECTION AND THE SITE FOOTER, WITH PURPLE/CYAN ACCENT CARDS INSIDE IT.
  - TEXT: HEADINGS `text-gray-800` (≈#1D2939); BODY `text-gray-500` (≈#667085); ON DARK SURFACES `text-white/90` AND `text-gray-400`.
- **PALETTE (DARK MODE)**: BODY BACKGROUND FLIPS TO `dark-secondary` (≈#101828), HEADER/CARDS TO `dark-primary` (≈#1D2939 / #182235), BORDERS TO `gray-800`, TEXT TO `white/90` FOR HEADINGS AND `gray-400` FOR BODY COPY. THE THEME TOGGLE BUTTON SWAPS A SUN ICON (LIGHT) FOR A MOON ICON (DARK) VIA `dark:` TAILWIND VARIANTS ON TWO STACKED SVGS.
- **TYPE SCALE**: HERO H1 ~40–44PX BOLD; SECTION H2 ~28–30PX (`text-3xl`/`md:text-title-lg`) BOLD, CENTERED; CARD/FORM LABELS ~14–16PX MEDIUM; BODY COPY 16PX/24PX LINE HEIGHT; SMALL META TEXT 14PX.
- **RADII**: PILL BUTTONS AND NAV LINKS `rounded-full`; CARDS `rounded-[20px]`; INPUTS `rounded-full`; SMALL BADGES/CHIPS `rounded-full`.
- **SHADOWS**: CARDS USE A SOFT `shadow-one`/`shadow-theme-xs` (LOW-OPACITY, LARGE-BLUR DROP SHADOW); THE PRICING TOGGLE PILL AND FAQ CONTAINER USE A SUBTLE INSET-LOOKING SHADOW.

## INTERACTIONS TO REPRODUCE
- **THEME TOGGLE**: HEADER ICON BUTTON TOGGLES A `dark` CLASS ON `<html>`/ROOT, SWAPPING ALL `dark:` TAILWIND-STYLE OVERRIDES; PERSIST CHOICE IN `localStorage` AND HONOR `prefers-color-scheme` ON FIRST LOAD (NO-FLASH BOOT SCRIPT).
- **NAV DROPDOWNS** (`Products`, `Pages`): CLICK/HOVER OPENS A ROUNDED WHITE/DARK CARD PANEL BELOW THE TRIGGER WITH A FADE+SLIDE-DOWN TRANSITION; CHEVRON ICON ROTATES 180°; CLOSES ON OUTSIDE CLICK OR ESCAPE.
- **MOBILE MENU**: HAMBURGER BUTTON (VISIBLE BELOW `lg`) TOGGLES A SLIDE-DOWN/OVERLAY PANEL LISTING ALL NAV ITEMS STACKED VERTICALLY.
- **FAQ ACCORDION**: CLICKING A QUESTION ROW TOGGLES `aria-expanded`, SLIDES THE ANSWER OPEN/CLOSED, AND SWAPS THE TRAILING ICON BETWEEN PLUS AND MINUS. ONLY ONE ITEM (THE FIRST) IS OPEN BY DEFAULT.
- **PRICING TOGGLE**: `Monthly`/`Annually` SEGMENTED CONTROL — ACTIVE SEGMENT HAS A DARK PILL BACKGROUND; SWITCHING RE-RENDERS PRICE AND BILLING PERIOD TEXT ON ALL FOUR CARDS.
- **TESTIMONIALS "SHOW MORE"**: CLICK REVEALS ADDITIONAL TESTIMONIAL CARDS BELOW THE FOLD AND REMOVES THE BOTTOM FADE GRADIENT MASK.
- **PASSWORD VISIBILITY TOGGLE**: EYE/EYE-SLASH ICON INSIDE PASSWORD FIELDS (SIGN IN/SIGN UP) TOGGLES `type="password"` ↔ `type="text"`.
- **BUTTON HOVERS**: GRADIENT BUTTONS ANIMATE `background-position` 0%→100% ON HOVER (0.6S EASE-IN-OUT) PLUS A SLIGHT SCALE/TRANSFORM; GHOST/OUTLINE BUTTONS AND NAV PILLS TRANSITION TEXT/BACKGROUND COLOR OVER 200MS; CARDS LIFT SLIGHTLY WITH A SOFT SHADOW INCREASE ON HOVER.
- **SCROLL/ENTRANCE**: SECTIONS FADE/SLIDE INTO VIEW ON SCROLL (SUBTLE, SHORT-DISTANCE TRANSLATE + OPACITY) FOR HERO CHIPS, FEATURE CARDS, AND TESTIMONIAL CARDS.
