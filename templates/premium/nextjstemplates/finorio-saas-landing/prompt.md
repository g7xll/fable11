## SUMMARY

FINORIO IS A CLEAN, MODERN FINANCE-APP MARKETING SITE BUILT ON A LIGHT LAVENDER / OFF-WHITE PALETTE WITH A ROUNDED GEOMETRIC DISPLAY FONT ("STACK SANS TEXT"). THE SITE CENTERS ON A FLOATING PILL NAVBAR, A HERO WITH A PHONE MOCKUP AND APP-STORE BADGES, FEATURE GRIDS WITH SOFT GRADIENT CARDS, ALTERNATING IMAGE/TEXT FEATURE ROWS, A PRICING SECTION WITH A MONTHLY/ANNUALLY TOGGLE, A TESTIMONIAL GRID, A FAQ ACCORDION, AND A LAVENDER CTA PANEL WITH ANGLED PHONE MOCKUPS. SEVEN PAGES TOTAL: HOME, FEATURES, PRICING, CONTACT, LOGIN, REGISTER, FORGET-PASSWORD. SHARED CHROME = THE PILL HEADER AND THE LIGHT FOOTER (PRODUCT / OTHERS / DOWNLOAD COLUMNS, SOCIAL ICONS, COPYRIGHT BAR).
