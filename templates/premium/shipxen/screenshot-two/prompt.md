## SUMMARY

SCREENSHOT TWO IS A SINGLE-PAGE MARKETING SITE FOR A FICTIONAL SCREENSHOT API. IT USES A BRIGHT SKY-BLUE / INDIGO ACCENT SYSTEM OVER A WHITE BASE WITH ALTERNATING SOFT-INDIGO ("FANCY OVERLAY") BAND SECTIONS. THE DISPLAY FONT IS DM SERIF DISPLAY AND THE BODY FONT IS DM SANS. THE PAGE OPENS WITH A FLOATING HEADER, A CENTERED HERO HEADLINE + A LARGE BROWSER/APP MOCKUP, A PRODUCT-HUNT BADGE ROW WITH FLOATING TESTIMONIAL AVATAR CHIPS, THEN A SERIES OF ALTERNATING FEATURE SECTIONS (IMAGE-LEFT / IMAGE-RIGHT) WITH THREE-ITEM SUB-FEATURE LISTS, A FULL-WIDTH INDIGO CTA BAND, A VIDEO "AUTOMATE AND ELEVATE" SECTION, A MASONRY TESTIMONIAL GRID, A RADIX-STYLE FAQ ACCORDION, AND A MULTI-COLUMN FOOTER. IT IS FULLY THEME-DEPENDENT (LIGHT + DARK) WITH A TOGGLE.
