About us

Building excellence through expertise

We are a full-service construction firm with two decades of experience delivering complex projects across commercial, residential, and infrastructure sectors. Our reputation is built on disciplined execution and lasting client relationships.

Our story

Our mission is to build with purpose and precision

Founded in 2004, Bastion began as a specialist structural contractor and has grown into a multi-disciplinary construction firm trusted by developers, public authorities, and global corporations alike.

We believe the best buildings come from the best processes — rigorous planning, honest communication, and teams that take ownership from the first drawing to the final handover. Every project we take on reflects that conviction.

What sets us apart

  • Transparent forecasting

    Honest budgets and schedules from day one, with no surprises at handover.

  • Field-led quality control

    Our superintendents own quality on site — punchlists start on day one, not at closeout.

  • Long-term relationships

    Over 70% of our annual revenue comes from repeat clients and referrals.

  • Safety-first culture

    An industry-leading safety record built through training, accountability, and genuine care for our people.
