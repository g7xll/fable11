Álvaro Ruiz

About

Álvaro Ruiz stewards relationships with developers and owners, aligning scopes, budgets, and schedules before a shovel hits the ground.

He frames projects with measurable outcomes, keeps stakeholders informed through each phase, and ensures delivery teams have the information they need to execute without surprises.

Álvaro believes disciplined communication and honest forecasting are the foundation of long-term construction partnerships. With over two decades leading major commercial, civic, and mixed-use developments across Europe and North America, he founded Bastion to build a firm where delivery integrity and client trust are non-negotiable standards.
