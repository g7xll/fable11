02 FAQ

Questions? We've got answers.

Everything you need to know before starting—whether you're new to Astro or just new to this course.

Do I need any prior experience with Astro?

Nope! This course is beginner-friendly and starts from the ground up. All you need is basic HTML and CSS knowledge.

How long will I have access to the course?

You'll get lifetime access to all course materials, including any future updates we make to keep content current.

Do I get a certificate after completing the course?

Yes! Once you complete all the lessons and exercises, you'll receive a certificate of completion you can share on LinkedIn or add to your portfolio.

Can I use this with my team?

Absolutely. The Teams plan is designed for collaborative learning, with shared progress tracking and an admin dashboard.

What if I don't like the course?

We offer a 14-day refund policy, no questions asked. Just reach out to our support team.

What tools do I need to follow along?

You'll need a code editor like VS Code, Node.js installed on your machine, and a basic understanding of the terminal.

Is this course up to date with the latest version of Astro?

Yes. We actively update the course content whenever Astro releases major updates to ensure everything stays relevant.

Can I preview the course before buying?

We offer a few free lessons so you can get a feel for the teaching style and content before committing.

Do you cover deployment?

Yes, we'll guide you through deploying your Astro site to popular platforms like Vercel and Netlify.

How long does the course take to complete?

It depends on your pace, but most students complete it in 4–6 hours of focused learning.
