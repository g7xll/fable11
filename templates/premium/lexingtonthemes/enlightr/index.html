Everything you need to build modern, high-performance websites

Enlightr gives you a structured path through Astro's most powerful features — from content collections and component islands to deployment pipelines and performance optimization. Whether you're migrating from React or starting fresh, this course meets you where you are and takes you where you want to go. No fluff, no filler — just the knowledge you need to ship real projects with confidence.

02 Who It's For

Built for builders who care about performance

If you've ever felt frustrated by slow builds, bloated JavaScript bundles, or framework lock-in — this course was designed with you in mind.

New to Astro?

We start from scratch so you never feel lost. Every concept is explained before it's applied, and every project builds on the last.

Frontend devs who want leaner builds

If you're used to React or Vue and want to ship less JavaScript by default, Astro's island architecture will change how you think about the web.

Designers and indie makers

You don't need to be a hardcore engineer. If you understand HTML and CSS, Astro lets you build powerful, production-ready sites without a backend.

Content creators & bloggers

Astro's content collections are a game changer for anyone managing lots of markdown. We'll show you how to build a blazing-fast blog or docs site.

Framework-curious folks

Astro plays nicely with React, Vue, Svelte, and more. If you're curious about the new wave of meta-frameworks, this is the perfect entry point.

Build real projects, not just examples

Every lesson is grounded in a real-world use case. You'll ship a blog, a product landing page, and a documentation site — all before the course ends.

Set your own pace

Lifetime access means you learn on your schedule. Revisit any lesson whenever you need a refresher — no expiry dates, no pressure.

Start from scratch, step by step

We assume nothing but basic web knowledge. Each video walks you through every command, every config, every decision — with clear reasoning.

Finish strong with a certificate

Complete all lessons and earn a certificate of completion you can share on LinkedIn or attach to your portfolio to show potential clients or employers.

Access forever — updates included

Astro ships fast. When new versions land, we update the course. You get access to every future lesson without paying again.

03 Course Experience

A learning experience designed to get you shipping

We cut the filler and focused on what matters. Our curriculum is built around projects, not lectures — so you spend more time building and less time watching.
