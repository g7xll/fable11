About Figma

Figma is the leading collaborative interface design tool. Figma connects everyone in the design process so teams can deliver better products, faster. We believe the best ideas emerge when people can work together seamlessly, regardless of where they are.

Since launching in 2016, Figma has become the go-to tool for design teams at companies like Airbnb, GitHub, Dropbox, Twitter, and thousands more. Our platform enables real-time collaboration, making design accessible to everyone on the team — not just designers.

Culture & Values

  • Collaboration first: We build tools for collaboration because we believe it makes work better.
  • Inclusive design: We design for everyone, which means designing with everyone.
  • Move fast, ship quality: We ship quickly without cutting corners on craft.
  • Be kind: We treat each other, our users, and our community with respect.
