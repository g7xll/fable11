Follow our journey and keep track of us.

Product updates, improvements, and new features delivered regularly.

v2.4.0

Multi-chain dashboard & ZK proof support

  • Native support for verifying ZK proofs from Noir and Circom circuits

Base chain support & performance improvements

Smart contract templates library
