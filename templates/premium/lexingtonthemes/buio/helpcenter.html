Frequently Asked Questions

What blockchains does Buio support?
Buio supports Ethereum, Arbitrum, Optimism, Polygon, Base, Avalanche, and all EVM-compatible chains. Solana support is currently in beta.
Is there a free plan?
Yes. Our Starter plan is free forever with 1 project and 100 transactions per month. No credit card required to get started.
How does self-custody work?
Your private keys never leave your device. Buio uses cryptographic signatures for authentication — we verify you control a key without ever seeing it. We have zero access to your funds or assets.
Can I cancel my subscription at any time?
Yes, you can cancel anytime. If you cancel a paid plan, you'll retain access until the end of your billing period, then automatically move to the free plan.
