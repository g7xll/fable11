Carrington Attorneys Featured in National Law Review

By the Carrington Law Editorial Team

Carrington Law is proud to announce that three of our attorneys have been featured in the National Law Review's annual legal spotlight — one of the most respected practitioner recognition programs in the American legal industry. The recognition affirms what our clients already know: Carrington Law's team brings extraordinary depth, precision, and client commitment to every matter they handle, regardless of complexity or scale.

About the National Law Review Recognition

The National Law Review's annual spotlight is one of the most widely read practitioner recognition programs in American legal publishing. Each year, the NLR editorial board selects attorneys from practices across the country based on a combination of peer nominations, client testimonials, published legal scholarship, and documented case outcomes. Being featured in the spotlight places an attorney among a select cohort recognized for their measurable impact on the legal community — not merely their years of experience or institutional affiliation.

For a law firm to have multiple attorneys featured in the same annual edition is genuinely uncommon. It speaks directly to the culture of excellence Carrington has cultivated across practice areas. This year's spotlight features attorneys recognized in intellectual property, mergers and acquisitions, and family law — three disciplines that anchor the firm's broader service offering and represent some of our most complex, consequential client work.

Featured Attorneys

Sarah Chen — Intellectual Property Law

Sarah Chen, a leading figure in intellectual property law with more than twenty years of experience protecting innovative companies, was recognized for her work in the emerging field of AI-related IP litigation. Her practice has expanded significantly to address the novel questions of ownership, authorship, and infringement that arise when artificial intelligence tools are used in the creation of copyrightable works. "Intellectual property law is in a period of genuine transformation," Sarah remarked upon learning of the recognition. "The questions we're being asked to answer today didn't exist five years ago, and the answers we develop now will define the landscape for the next generation of innovators. I'm honored that the NLR recognizes the importance of this work."

John Sullivan — Mergers & Acquisitions

John Sullivan, a seasoned corporate attorney specializing in mergers and acquisitions, was featured for his leadership on several cross-border transactions that closed in the past year — including a landmark $250 million acquisition involving entities in three jurisdictions. John is known for his ability to structure deals that satisfy the commercial objectives of all stakeholders while navigating the regulatory complexity that increasingly characterizes international M&A. "Great deal work is about more than documents," John said. "It's about understanding what the client is actually trying to build and making sure the transaction is the right vehicle for that goal. I'm grateful for the recognition, but the credit belongs to every client who trusted us with their most important business decisions."

Emily Johnson — Family Law

Emily Johnson, Carrington's compassionate and tenacious family law attorney, was recognized for her dedication to helping families navigate high-stakes custody, divorce, and guardianship matters with dignity and clarity. Emily's practice reflects a philosophy that legal representation in family matters should be as humane as it is effective — that clients in the middle of difficult life transitions deserve both strategic advocacy and genuine human support. "Family law is unlike any other practice," Emily shared. "The outcomes we secure don't just resolve legal questions — they shape families for years, sometimes for generations. I take that responsibility seriously, and I'm honored to be recognized for the commitment our team brings to every case."

About the Firm

Carrington Law was founded on the belief that rigorous legal counsel and genuine client partnership are not in tension — they are the same thing. Over more than two decades, the firm has grown from a boutique litigation practice to a full-service law firm with deep capabilities in corporate law, intellectual property, employment, family law, criminal defense, and commercial real estate. Carrington attorneys are regularly recognized by national and regional publications for their contributions to the development of the law and their exceptional service to clients.

The firm's reputation rests not on the size of its client roster, but on the consistency with which it delivers results across the full spectrum of client needs. Whether advising a Fortune 500 company on a cross-border acquisition or representing an individual in a custody dispute, Carrington brings the same level of preparation, strategic thinking, and personal attention.

Looking Ahead

This recognition from the National Law Review comes at a moment when Carrington is expanding its practice capabilities to address emerging client needs in areas including technology law, environmental compliance, and cross-border dispute resolution. The firm is committed to recruiting and retaining attorneys who combine substantive expertise with genuine investment in their clients' success — and to maintaining the culture of excellence that has made this recognition possible.

We congratulate Sarah, John, and Emily on this well-deserved recognition, and we extend our deepest thanks to the clients whose trust has made our work meaningful.
