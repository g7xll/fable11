Carrington Law Expands Practice Areas

Carrington Law is pleased to announce the formal expansion of its practice areas, reflecting the firm's ongoing commitment to meeting the evolving legal needs of our clients. Effective immediately, the firm has established dedicated practice groups in two rapidly growing fields: technology and data privacy law, and environmental and regulatory compliance. These additions bring Carrington's total number of active practice areas to twelve, cementing our standing as one of the most comprehensive full-service law firms in the region. The expansion follows extensive consultation with existing clients who identified gaps between their emerging legal needs and the services previously available in-house.

The technology and data privacy group will be led by a team of attorneys with deep expertise in cybersecurity incident response, cross-border data transfer frameworks, and compliance with state and federal privacy statutes including the California Consumer Privacy Act and its successors. As data breaches and regulatory enforcement actions become more frequent, businesses require counsel who not only understand the law but can move quickly to contain exposure and implement lasting protective measures. Our environmental and regulatory practice will focus on advising clients facing permitting challenges, enforcement proceedings from the EPA and state agencies, and the growing set of obligations tied to ESG disclosure requirements.

This expansion underscores Carrington's belief that the law firm of the future must grow alongside the industries it serves. By investing proactively in practice areas that align with emerging legal complexity, we ensure that clients have access to specialized guidance without needing to engage multiple firms. We look forward to welcoming clients with needs in these new areas and to providing the same standard of rigorous, personalized counsel that has defined Carrington Law for over two decades.

Carrington Law provides comprehensive legal services with a commitment to excellence, integrity, and client success.
