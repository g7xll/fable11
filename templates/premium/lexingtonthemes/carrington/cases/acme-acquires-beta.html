Acme Corp Acquires Beta Ltd.

June 15, 2025 · Acme Corp

Carrington Law served as lead counsel for Acme Corp in a landmark $250 million cross-border acquisition of Beta Ltd., one of the most complex corporate transactions our firm has handled in the technology sector. The engagement required coordinating simultaneous due diligence workstreams across three jurisdictions — the United States, the United Kingdom, and Singapore — while managing an extraordinarily compressed timeline driven by competitive market pressures. Our team assembled a multidisciplinary group of attorneys spanning corporate finance, antitrust, employment, intellectual property, and data privacy to ensure comprehensive coverage of every material risk.

A central challenge in this transaction was structuring the deal to satisfy divergent regulatory requirements without triggering merger notification thresholds in the EU. Our antitrust team conducted a detailed competitive overlap analysis and worked proactively with regulators in each jurisdiction to pre-clear concerns before formal filings. We also negotiated robust representations and warranties insurance to protect Acme from post-closing indemnification risk, allowing the parties to agree to a cleaner purchase price adjustment mechanism and a shorter escrow period than initially proposed by Beta's counsel.

The intellectual property and technology component of the transaction required particular care. Beta Ltd. held a portfolio of twenty-three registered patents, eleven pending applications, and a suite of proprietary software products licensed across a global customer base. Our IP team conducted a freedom-to-operate analysis, reviewed all material license agreements for change-of-control provisions, and negotiated assignments that preserved Acme's ability to sublicense the acquired technology without seeking third-party consents. We also advised on the structure of employee retention agreements for Beta's core engineering team, whose continued employment was essential to the strategic value of the deal.

The transaction closed on schedule and within budget, delivering Acme Corp full ownership of Beta Ltd. and its global operations. The successful $250 million acquisition positioned Acme as the dominant provider in its market segment and added significant recurring revenue to its balance sheet. Carrington Law continues to advise Acme Corp on post-closing integration matters, including the harmonization of employment policies, data protection frameworks, and corporate governance structures across the combined entity.
