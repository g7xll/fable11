Event Details

What time should we arrive for the ceremony?

Please arrive 10–15 minutes before the ceremony starts to find your seat.

Is there a dress code?

Semi-formal. Think suits, dresses, and cocktail attire.

Will the ceremony be indoors or outdoors?

The ceremony will be outdoors. Shade will be provided. Flat or wedge footwear is recommended.

Travel & Accommodation

Where should we stay?

We recommend the Hillside Hotel and Lakeview Inn, both within 10 minutes of the venue.

Is parking available at the venue?

Yes, free parking is available at both the ceremony and reception venues.

Food & Drink

Are dietary restrictions accommodated?

Yes! Please indicate your dietary requirements in your RSVP and we'll make arrangements.

Will there be a vegetarian option?

Absolutely. We'll have vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options available at all meals.

Photos & Gifts

Can we take photos during the ceremony?

We're having an unplugged ceremony — please keep phones and cameras put away during the vows. You'll have plenty of opportunities to snap photos at the reception!

Do you have a gift registry?

Yes! You can browse our wishlist here. Your presence is the greatest gift, but if you'd like to contribute, we'd be grateful.
