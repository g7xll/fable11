Overview

Mesa Health asked us to reduce churn in their patient portal and give clinicians a clearer view of daily capacity. We redesigned the entire experience, from appointment booking to post-visit follow-up, with a focus on clarity and accessibility.

Objectives

  • Simplify the patient booking flow and reduce drop-off.
  • Give clinicians a unified dashboard for scheduling and messages.
  • Establish a design ops cadence the internal team could maintain.

Approach

We conducted contextual research with 12 patients and 8 clinicians, mapped the existing flows, and identified the top five friction points. From there, we redesigned the booking flow, rebuilt the dashboard information architecture, and created a component library the internal team now maintains.

Results

  • Booking drop-off reduced by 31%.
  • Clinician daily task completion time dropped by 22%.
  • Portal satisfaction score increased from 3.2 to 4.6 out of 5.
