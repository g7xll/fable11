Accessibility quick wins before launch

By Alex Chen • September 15, 2025

Ship fast while covering the essentials: focus, contrast, and keyboard flow.

You don't need to achieve WCAG AAA perfection before you launch—but these quick wins will cover the most common accessibility failures and serve the largest group of users.

Focus management

  • Every interactive element must have a visible focus indicator (not just the browser default).
  • Skip-to-content links help keyboard users bypass navigation.
  • Modals and dialogs must trap focus while open and return it on close.

Color contrast

  • Normal text: 4.5:1 minimum ratio (WCAG AA).
  • Large text (18pt+ or 14pt bold): 3:1 minimum.
  • UI components and focus indicators: 3:1 minimum against adjacent colors.

Keyboard navigation

  1. Tab through the entire page—every interactive element should be reachable.
  2. Test forms, dropdowns, and modals with keyboard only.
  3. Verify that custom components (accordions, tabs) expose correct ARIA roles.
