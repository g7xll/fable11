Elm District

Quiet residential area with tree-lined streets

Elm District is known for its peaceful atmosphere, family-friendly parks, and easy access to schools, grocery stores, and public transport. The area features a mix of modern homes and beautifully preserved early 20th-century architecture, creating a charming streetscape unlike any other in the city.

The neighborhood's elm-lined boulevards are particularly beautiful in autumn, and the central square hosts a farmers market every Saturday morning. With property values appreciating steadily over the past decade, Elm District is considered one of the best investment areas in the city.
