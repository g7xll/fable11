Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 25, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Westend Oy ("Westend", "we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects the personal information you provide when using our services and website.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you contact us, request a valuation, sign up for our newsletter, or book a consultation. This may include your name, email address, phone number, property address, and financial information relevant to your real estate needs.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide real estate services and respond to your enquiries
  • To send property listings and market updates relevant to your stated interests
  • To conduct property valuations and market analyses
  • To communicate service updates, changes, and important information
  • To comply with legal obligations under Finnish and EU law

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our website or conducting our business, subject to confidentiality agreements. We may disclose information where required by law.

GDPR Rights

If you are located in the European Union, you have the right to access, correct, delete, and restrict processing of your personal data. You may also object to processing and request data portability. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@westend.example.com.

Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies to ensure proper functionality. We may also use analytics cookies to understand how visitors use our site. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

Contact

For privacy-related enquiries, please contact our Data Protection Officer at Westend Oy, Mannerheimintie 123, 00100 Helsinki, Finland, or email privacy@westend.example.com.
