About Westend

A premier Nordic real estate agency founded on expertise, integrity, and an unwavering commitment to client success.

Our Story

Founded in Helsinki, Built on Trust

Westend was established in 2015 with a singular mission: to bring transparency, expertise, and exceptional service to Nordic real estate. What began as a small boutique agency in Helsinki has grown into a trusted partner for buyers, sellers, and investors across Scandinavia and the Baltic region.

Our team of 40+ specialists brings deep local knowledge of each Nordic market, combined with international experience to serve clients relocating from abroad. We believe that buying or selling a home should be an empowering experience — not a stressful one.

In a decade of operation, we have completed over 500 transactions, maintained a 98% client satisfaction rate, and built a reputation for honest, transparent guidance that puts client interests first at every stage.
