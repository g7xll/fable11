## SUMMARY

WESTEND IS A PREMIUM NORDIC-STYLE REAL ESTATE TEMPLATE BY LEXINGTON THEMES. IT IS A COMPREHENSIVE MULTI-PAGE SITE FOR A LUXURY PROPERTY AGENCY OPERATING ACROSS SCANDINAVIAN CITIES. THE DESIGN IS MINIMAL, CLEAN, AND TYPOGRAPHICALLY DRIVEN WITH GENEROUS WHITESPACE, A NEAR-MONOCHROME PALETTE, AND SUBTLE HOVER ANIMATIONS.

THE TEMPLATE COVERS THE COMPLETE REAL ESTATE WORKFLOW: PROPERTY LISTINGS (FOR SALE, FOR RENT, OFF-MARKET, SOLD), OPEN HOUSES, AGENTS, NEIGHBORHOODS, SERVICES, BLOG, CAREERS, RESOURCES, VALUATION, CONTACT, AND LEGAL PAGES. IT ALSO INCLUDES A SYSTEM OVERVIEW PAGE.
