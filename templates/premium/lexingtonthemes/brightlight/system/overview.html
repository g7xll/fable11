Incident History

May 15, 2025

Elevated SMTP latency in EU region

A misconfigured load balancer caused elevated delivery times (avg. 8s vs 45ms) for European senders for approximately 38 minutes. No emails were lost. The issue was resolved after a config rollback.

Mar 3, 2025

Webhook delivery delays

A queue backlog caused webhook delivery delays of 2–15 minutes for roughly 12% of customers. The backlog was cleared within 22 minutes. All webhooks were eventually delivered.

Jan 18, 2025

Brief API unavailability

A database connection pool exhaustion caused 503 errors on the sending API for 4 minutes. Email sends queued during this window were delivered immediately after recovery.
