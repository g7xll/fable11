Frequently asked questions

Is the lens interchangeable?

+

No, the X100V has a fixed 23mm f/2.0 lens that cannot be swapped. This fixed design allows Fujifilm to optimize the lens and sensor pairing for exceptional image quality and a compact form factor that wouldn't be possible with an interchangeable lens system.

Does it support external microphones?

+

Yes, it includes a 2.5mm mic input that allows you to connect an external microphone for improved audio quality when recording video. You may need a 2.5mm to 3.5mm adapter for most standard microphones.

Is it weather-sealed?

+

Yes, when used with the optional adapter ring and 49mm protective filter, the X100V offers weather and dust resistance, allowing you to shoot confidently in light rain or dusty environments.
