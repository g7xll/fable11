Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before joining—especially if you're new to design engineering or shifting from a pure design or dev background.

Do I need a design or development background?

Not necessarily. The course starts with foundational design-engineering concepts and builds up. If you're comfortable with HTML/CSS and want to bridge design and frontend, you're ready.

How long do I keep access to the course?

You get lifetime access. Enroll once and revisit lessons, project files, and future updates anytime you want.

Will I get a certificate?

Yes! Once you complete all core lessons and projects, you'll get a downloadable certificate to showcase your skills.

Can my whole team join?

Definitely. The Teams plan includes multi-seat access, shared dashboards, and team progress tracking tools.

What if the course isn't for me?

No worries. You're covered by a 14-day money-back guarantee. Just email us and we'll refund you—no hard feelings.

What tools will I need?

You'll need Figma (or a design tool you're comfortable with), a code editor like VS Code, and a modern browser. We'll guide you through everything else step-by-step.

Is this up to date with current design system practices?

Yes. The course is regularly updated to reflect current best practices in design systems, component libraries, and frontend tooling.

Can I try it before buying?

Yes. We've included a few free lessons so you can get a feel for the format and teaching style before enrolling.

Do you cover real-world deployment?

Absolutely. You'll learn how to ship production-ready design systems using platforms like Vercel and GitHub.

How long will the course take to complete?

Most learners complete it in 3–5 weeks at a steady pace, but it's entirely self-paced—go faster or take your time.
