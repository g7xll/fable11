Senior Architect

New York, NY full time $90,000 - $130,000
Apply Now

Join our New York studio as a Senior Architect and take a leadership role in the design and delivery of complex, high-profile projects. You will mentor junior staff, drive design excellence, and serve as a trusted advisor to our clients.

Requirements

  • 10+ years of architectural experience
  • Licensed architect (AIA preferred)
  • Strong portfolio of built works
  • Excellent leadership and communication skills
