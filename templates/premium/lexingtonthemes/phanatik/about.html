About

Our Story

Phanatik was born out of a belief that news should be both informative and engaging. We set out to create a platform where quality journalism meets modern design — delivering stories that matter to a global audience hungry for clarity in a noisy world.

Founded in 2023 by a team of journalists and technologists, we've grown into a trusted voice across business, technology, sports, and culture.

Our Vision

We envision a world where everyone has access to fearless, independent journalism. Our goal is to build the most trusted news brand for the next generation — one that doesn't just report facts, but helps readers understand the forces shaping their lives.

From breaking news to in-depth analysis, Phanatik is committed to holding power accountable and amplifying voices that deserve to be heard.

The Media Landscape Has Changed — So Have We

In an era of algorithmic feeds, clickbait headlines, and misinformation, Phanatik stands for something different. We believe that great journalism requires time, resources, and above all, integrity. Every story we publish is the result of rigorous reporting, careful editing, and a commitment to the truth — however uncomfortable it may be.

We launched Phanatik because we saw a gap in the market. Most news outlets were racing to publish faster, chasing page views and social shares rather than accuracy and depth. We decided to do the opposite: slow down, dig deeper, and deliver reporting that actually moves the needle.

What We Cover

Our editorial coverage spans the full spectrum of modern life. In the Business section, our team tracks the forces reshaping global commerce — from supply chain disruptions to the rise of decentralized finance and the boardroom battles defining tomorrow's economy.

In Technology, we don't just cover gadgets. We examine the social, ethical, and economic implications of the tools we're building — asking not just what AI can do, but what it should do. Our reporters attend the chip launches and the congressional hearings, so you don't have to.

Sports at Phanatik goes beyond the scoreline. We explore the business of athletics, the science of performance, and the cultural conversations that sport continues to spark. Whether it's the meteoric rise of esports or the fight for gender equity in professional leagues, we're there.

Our Health coverage prioritizes evidence-based reporting at a time when health misinformation is rife. We partner with medical professionals and researchers to bring you the science without the spin.

And in Finance, we help everyday readers navigate markets, personal wealth, and the macro forces that affect their savings, careers, and futures.

Our Editorial Standards

Every story published on Phanatik goes through a rigorous editorial process. We verify facts with primary sources wherever possible, offer subjects the opportunity to respond to significant claims, and correct errors transparently and promptly. Our editorial team is independent of our commercial operations — advertisers have no influence on what we publish.

We're proud to be a member of the Standards in Journalism Network and signatories of the Journalism Trust Initiative. Our readers are our most important constituency.

Who We Are

Phanatik is home to a team of award-winning journalists, designers, and engineers who believe that form and function belong together. Our newsroom is distributed across four continents, giving us a truly global perspective on events that matter.

Our founding editor, a former foreign correspondent with twenty years in the field, set the tone early: "We're here to inform, not to inflame. We respect our readers' intelligence." That philosophy guides every editorial decision we make.

We're reader-supported through subscriptions and thoughtfully chosen advertising partners. We don't answer to venture capital. We don't run programmatic pop-ups. We think good journalism is worth paying for — and we work every day to prove it.

Join the Conversation

Phanatik is more than a publication — it's a community. We host live events, publish newsletters, and produce a weekly podcast where our journalists go deeper on the biggest stories of the week. We'd love for you to be part of it.

Subscribe to stay informed. Share our work if it moves you. And if you spot something we got wrong — or something we should be covering — please reach out. The best journalism is a conversation, and we're listening.
