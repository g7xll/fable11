Stripe

Stripe builds economic infrastructure for the internet, powering online payments and financial services for businesses of all sizes.

Founded in 2010, Stripe has grown to process hundreds of billions of dollars in transactions annually and serves millions of businesses in more than 120 countries.

Our mission is to increase the GDP of the internet by making it easier for businesses to start, grow, and manage their online operations.
