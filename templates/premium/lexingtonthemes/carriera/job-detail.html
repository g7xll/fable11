Product Designer

About the Role

We're looking for a passionate and experienced Product Designer to join our remote team at Polar. You'll work closely with our product, engineering, and business teams to craft delightful user experiences that help open-source developers thrive financially.

Responsibilities

  • Own end-to-end product design for new features and improvements
  • Conduct user research and translate insights into intuitive designs
  • Create high-fidelity mockups and interactive prototypes
  • Collaborate with engineers to ensure pixel-perfect implementation
  • Contribute to and evolve our design system

Requirements

  • 3+ years of product design experience in B2B or developer tools
  • Strong portfolio demonstrating end-to-end product thinking
  • Proficiency in Figma
  • Experience with design systems
  • Excellent communication skills
