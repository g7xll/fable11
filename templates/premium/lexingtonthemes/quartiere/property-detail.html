About this property

Welcome to Beachfront Bliss, an extraordinary oceanfront residence perched along the legendary Pacific Coast Highway in the heart of Beverly Hills. This stunning 2,200-square-foot sanctuary offers an unparalleled blend of luxury living and natural coastal beauty.

Step inside to discover an open-concept floor plan bathed in natural light, where floor-to-ceiling windows frame panoramic Pacific Ocean views that stretch to the horizon. The chef's kitchen features Carrara marble countertops, Sub-Zero appliances, and a waterfall island perfect for entertaining.

The primary suite is a true retreat, featuring a private ocean-view terrace, spa-like bathroom with freestanding soaking tub, and a custom walk-in closet. Three additional bedrooms offer ample space for family and guests, each thoughtfully appointed with premium finishes.

Outdoor living takes center stage with an expansive deck, infinity pool that merges with the ocean, and direct beach access. The property also includes a two-car garage, smart home technology, and 24-hour security.

Located minutes from world-class dining, luxury shopping on Rodeo Drive, and the vibrant cultural scene of Los Angeles, this is truly coastal living at its finest.
