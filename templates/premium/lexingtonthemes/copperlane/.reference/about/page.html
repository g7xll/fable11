Service Experience Director

David Lee

David Lee designs the customer journey at Copperlane, from online scheduling to the handshake at pickup. He blends lean shop processes with digital tools so every appointment runs on time and every customer knows exactly what was done to their vehicle. He partners with technicians to translate complex repairs into clear updates, making sure estimates are transparent and approvals are simple. David also trains advisors on communication standards that keep customers informed without slowing down the bay. Before joining Copperlane, David modernized service lanes for several multi-location dealerships, improving CSI scores and same-day close rates.

Detailing & Protection Lead

Jukke Turner

Jukke Turner oversees detailing, paint correction, and ceramic protection at Copperlane. He is meticulous about prep work, ensuring every surface is decontaminated before any machine touches paint. Jukke designs service packages that balance gloss, durability, and budget, whether it's a quick refresh or a full multi-stage correction. He also trains the team on coating application, curing protocols, and interior restoration standards. With a background in luxury detailing studios, Jukke brings a craftsman's eye to daily driver and enthusiast vehicles alike.
