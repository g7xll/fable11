> A SELF-CONTAINED, PIXEL-FAITHFUL REPRODUCTION OF THE NOTION BLOG TEMPLATE — A NEXT.JS + NOTION-POWERED BLOG DEMO BUILT BY THE VERCEL/NEXT.JS TEAM. REFERENCE: `https://notion-blog.vercel.app/`

## SUMMARY

A MINIMAL, CLEAN BLOG TEMPLATE BUILT WITH NEXT.JS AND NOTION AS THE CMS BACKEND. THE SITE DEMONSTRATES STATICALLY GENERATED PAGES FROM NOTION CONTENT, DEPLOYED ON VERCEL. IT CONSISTS OF THREE PAGES: A HOME/LANDING PAGE SHOWCASING THE TEMPLATE'S FEATURES, A BLOG INDEX LISTING POSTS, AND A CONTACT PAGE FOR THE AUTHOR. THE DESIGN IS VERY MINIMAL — WHITE BACKGROUND WITH A SUBTLE POLKA-DOT PATTERN, SYSTEM FONT STACK, AND VERCEL'S GEIST-INSPIRED DESIGN TOKENS.
