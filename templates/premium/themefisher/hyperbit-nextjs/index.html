Frequently Asked Questions

Hyperbit offers branding design, product UI/UX design, social media ad creatives, presentation and slide deck design, and front-end development. We work as a subscription-based design partner for startups and growing businesses.

Most design requests are completed within 48 hours. Complex projects like full brand identities or product redesigns may take longer, but we'll always give you a clear timeline upfront.

We primarily work in Figma for design, and we integrate with your existing workflow tools — Slack for communication, Notion for project management, and Loom for async video feedback.

No contracts, no lock-ins. Our subscriptions are month-to-month and you can pause or cancel anytime. We believe the quality of our work is what keeps clients around.

Once you're a subscriber, you'll get access to our client portal where you can submit design requests, leave feedback, and track progress in real time. It's simple and streamlined.
