FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Your Trusted Partner in Data Protection with Cutting-Edge Solutions for Comprehensive Data Security.

1. What is two-factor authentication (2FA) and how does it enhance security?
If you receive a suspicious email, do not click on any links or download attachments. Verify the sender's email address and look for signs of phishing, such as misspellings or unusual requests. If in doubt, contact the sender directly using a known, trusted method of communication. Report the email to your IT department or email provider.
2. What should I do if I receive a suspicious email?
Never click links or download attachments from suspicious emails. Verify sender information, report to IT security, and delete the message. When in doubt, contact the apparent sender through a known phone number or official website to confirm legitimacy.
3. What is encryption and how does it protect my data?
Encryption converts your data into an unreadable format that can only be decoded with the correct key. Fortify uses AES-256 encryption — the same standard used by government agencies — to protect all your stored and transmitted data.
4. How can I ensure my mobile devices are secure?
Keep your operating system and apps updated, use strong lock screen passwords, enable remote wipe capabilities, only install apps from official stores, and use a VPN on public Wi-Fi networks. Fortify's mobile security features provide an additional layer of protection.
5. What are the common signs of a data breach?
Watch for unusual account activity, unexpected password changes, strange emails sent from your accounts, unfamiliar charges, or sluggish device performance. Fortify's real-time monitoring alerts you immediately if any suspicious activity is detected.
6. How can I create a strong password?
Use at least 12 characters with a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols. Avoid personal information and common words. Use a unique password for each account, and consider using a reputable password manager to generate and store complex passwords securely.
