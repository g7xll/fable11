Frequently Asked Questions

1.What is two-factor authentication (2FA) and how does it enhance security?
If you receive a suspicious email, do not click on any links or download attachments. Verify the sender's email address and look for signs of phishing, such as misspellings or unusual requests.
2.What should I do if I receive a suspicious email?
Never click links or download attachments from suspicious emails. Verify sender information, report to IT security, and delete the message.
3.What is encryption and how does it protect my data?
Encryption converts your data into an unreadable format that can only be decoded with the correct key. Fortify uses AES-256 encryption to protect all your stored and transmitted data.
4.How can I ensure my mobile devices are secure?
Keep your operating system and apps updated, use strong lock screen passwords, enable remote wipe capabilities, and only install apps from official stores.
5.What are the common signs of a data breach?
Watch for unusual account activity, unexpected password changes, strange emails sent from your accounts, or unfamiliar charges.
6.How can I create a strong password?
Use at least 12 characters with a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols. Use a unique password for each account and consider using a password manager.
