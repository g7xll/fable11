Connect with Your Favorite Tools

Fortify integrates seamlessly with the platforms and tools your team already uses, making security effortless to maintain.

GitHub

Amazon AWS

Monitor and secure your AWS infrastructure with deep integration into CloudTrail, GuardDuty, and Security Hub.

Google Workspace

Protect your Google Workspace environment with comprehensive monitoring of Gmail, Drive, and admin activities.
