> A SELF-CONTAINED, PIXEL-FAITHFUL REPRODUCTION OF THE BOOKWORM LIGHT NEXTJS THEME BY THEMEFISHER. ALL PAGES ARE STATIC HTML + CSS + VANILLA JS WITH NO BUILD STEP REQUIRED.
> REFERENCE: `https://themefisher.com/demo?theme=bookworm-light-nextjs`

## SUMMARY

BOOKWORM LIGHT IS A MINIMAL, TYPOGRAPHY-FOCUSED PERSONAL BLOG THEME BY THEMEFISHER, BUILT WITH NEXT.JS. IT FEATURES A LIGHT COLOR SCHEME WITH A TEAL ACCENT, A STICKY HEADER WITH A DROPDOWN "PAGES" MENU AND SEARCH MODAL, A MASONRY-STYLE POST FEED (ONE FEATURED POST FOLLOWED BY A TWO-COLUMN GRID), A DARK FOOTER, AND SUPPORTING PAGES FOR AUTHORS, CATEGORIES, TAGS, A MARKDOWN "ELEMENTS" STYLE GUIDE, A CONTACT FORM, AND A SINGLE POST TEMPLATE WITH TAGS, SOCIAL SHARE ICONS, AND A "SIMILAR POSTS" SECTION. THE CLONE REPRODUCES THE HOME, ABOUT, CONTACT, AUTHORS (+ 2 AUTHOR DETAIL PAGES), CATEGORIES (+ 5 CATEGORY DETAIL PAGES), TAGS, ELEMENTS, PRIVACY POLICY, AND 4 SINGLE-POST PAGES AS STANDALONE HTML FILES.

## LAYOUT & STRUCTURE

### SHARED CHROME (ALL PAGES)

- **HEADER**: LOGO LEFT, CENTERED NAV (HOME / ABOUT / CONTACT / PAGES DROPDOWN: AUTHORS, CATEGORIES, TAGS, ELEMENTS, PRIVACY POLICY), SEARCH ICON RIGHT OPENING A MODAL OVERLAY
- **FOOTER**: DARK NAVY BACKGROUND, CENTERED FOOTER NAV (ABOUT / CONTACT / ELEMENTS / PRIVACY POLICY), ROW OF 5 SOCIAL ICON BUTTONS (FACEBOOK, TWITTER, INSTAGRAM, YOUTUBE, LINKEDIN), COPYRIGHT LINE

### PAGES

1. **HOME (`index.html`)** — ONE FULL-WIDTH FEATURED POST CARD (IMAGE, AUTHOR META, TITLE, EXCERPT) FOLLOWED BY A TWO-COLUMN GRID OF SMALLER POST CARDS, THEN NUMBERED PAGINATION.
2. **ABOUT (`about.html`)** — FULL-WIDTH PORTRAIT IMAGE, CENTERED HEADLINE, SOCIAL ICON ROW, TWO CENTERED BIO PARAGRAPHS.
3. **CONTACT (`contact.html`)** — CENTERED "CONTACT" TITLE ABOVE A NAME/EMAIL/SUBJECT/MESSAGE FORM WITH A "SUBMIT NOW" OUTLINE BUTTON.
4. **AUTHORS (`authors.html`)** — GRID OF AUTHOR CARDS (AVATAR, NAME, BIO SNIPPET), LINKING TO PER-AUTHOR DETAIL PAGES (`authors-john-doe.html`, `authors-mark-dinn.html`) LISTING THAT AUTHOR'S POSTS.
5. **CATEGORIES (`categories.html`)** — ROW OF PILL LINKS (ART, DEVELOPMENT, LIFESTYLE, ACCESSORIES, FOOD) TO PER-CATEGORY DETAIL PAGES (`categories-*.html`) LISTING POSTS IN THAT CATEGORY.
6. **TAGS (`tags.html`)** — ROW OF PILL LINKS FOR POST TAGS.
7. **ELEMENTS (`elements.html`)** — MARKDOWN STYLE-GUIDE: HEADINGS 1–6, EMPHASIS/STRONG/STRIKETHROUGH, LINK EXAMPLES, A LONG PARAGRAPH, ORDERED/UNORDERED LISTS, CODE BLOCKS, BLOCKQUOTE, INLINE HTML NOTES, AND TWO TABLES.
8. **PRIVACY POLICY (`privacy-policy.html`)** — CENTERED TITLE OVER STANDARD PRIVACY-POLICY COPY WITH SUBHEADS.
9. **SINGLE POST (`post-1.html`, `post-3.html`, `post-4.html`, `post-7.html`)** — CENTERED TITLE + META, FULL-WIDTH COVER IMAGE, ARTICLE BODY WITH A SUBHEAD AND BLOCKQUOTE, TAG PILLS + SOCIAL SHARE ROW, AND A 3-UP "SIMILAR POSTS" GRID.
