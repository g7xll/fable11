## SUMMARY

GLASTO IS A MULTI-LAYOUT SAAS & STARTUP NEXT.JS TEMPLATE BY THEMEFISHER, SHOWCASING "SOCIALSYNC" — A SOCIAL MEDIA MANAGEMENT PLATFORM. THE TEMPLATE IS A LIGHT-MODE-FIRST DESIGN (WITH DARK SECTIONS) USING A BOLD BLUE PRIMARY (#3A7DFF) AGAINST A DEEP NAVY DARK (#161C52), BODY TEXT IN SLATE-GREY (#484A61), AND A CLEAN WHITE BACKGROUND. IT FEATURES 16 DISTINCT PAGES (HOME, ELEMENTS, COMPANY, PRICING, INTEGRATION, CASE STUDIES, CASE STUDY DETAILS, CONTACT, REQUEST A DEMO, CHANGELOG, BLOG, BLOG DETAILS, REVIEWS, PRIVACY POLICY, TERMS & CONDITIONS, AND 404), EACH SHARING A COMMON HEADER/NAV (WITH AN "ALL PAGES" MEGA-DROPDOWN) AND FOOTER. ENTRANCE ANIMATIONS ARE POWERED BY AOS (ANIMATE ON SCROLL); SWIPER.JS DRIVES TESTIMONIAL AND LOGO CAROUSELS; LENIS-STYLE SMOOTH SCROLL IS USED FOR THE OVERALL PAGE EXPERIENCE.
