About Seoptic

We're on a mission to make intelligent SEO accessible and actionable for every business, everywhere.

Our Mission

Built to make SEO simple, smart, and scalable

Seoptic was founded in 2021 by a team of SEO engineers and data scientists who were frustrated with tools that were either too simple or too complex. We set out to build an SEO platform that combines cutting-edge AI with a clean, intuitive interface.

Today, more than 10,000 marketers, agencies, and businesses rely on Seoptic every day to monitor their rankings, audit their sites, and grow organic traffic — without needing a PhD in search algorithms.
