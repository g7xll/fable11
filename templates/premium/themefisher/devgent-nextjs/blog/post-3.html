Overview

Modern teams are overwhelmed with repetitive tasks from writing proposals to summarizing reports and switching between tools. While these tasks are necessary, they steal time away from high-impact work. In this post, we’ll show how real teams are using Devgent to automate those time-wasting tasks, reclaim their productivity, and focus on what actually matters.

The hidden cost of busywork

What’s slowing teams down isn’t just the volume of work—it’s the nature of it. Marketing teams build the same reports over and over. Sales teams repeat tasks that AI could handle in seconds. Admins spend hours gathering data that could be auto-synced.

“The best work doesn’t come from doing more—it comes from doing what matters. Devgent clears the noise so teams can focus on what truly moves them forward.”
— Liam Patel, Product Strategist

How Devgent automates smarter

Devgent steps in where traditional tools stop with intelligence, speed, and simplicity. From auto-generating proposals to summarizing long emails, Devgent eliminates the need to do everything manually.

The marketing team story
