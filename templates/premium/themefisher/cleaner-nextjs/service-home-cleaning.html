Service overview

A neatly maintained home speaks for your family

A neatly maintained home is an important asset to every household. It reflects who you are and influences how your guests perceive you. Our home cleaning turnaround time depends on the size and condition of your home — we work in teams of 2-4 or more led by an experienced team leader.

  • Deep kitchen & bath cleaning

    Hospital-grade disinfectants remove bacteria and grime from the rooms that need it most.

  • Dusting & surface care

    Every shelf, ledge and appliance is wiped down and left streak-free from top to bottom.

  • Eco-friendly products

    Biodegradable, low-VOC cleaning agents keep your home safe for family and pets.

  • Flexible scheduling

    One-time deep cleans or recurring maintenance plans built around your household's routine.
