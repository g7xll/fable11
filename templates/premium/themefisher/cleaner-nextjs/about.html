About Auburn Cleaning Co.

We deliver spotless results, every single time.

Auburn Cleaning Co. has spent over a decade perfecting reliable, eco-conscious cleaning for homes and businesses. Our mission is simple: give you back your time with a space that feels brand new.

From a single home-cleaning crew to a full-service company covering floor care, pest control, and window cleaning, our story has always been about people first — trained, trusted, and equipped with innovative technology to get the job done right.

  • Eco-friendly cleaning products
