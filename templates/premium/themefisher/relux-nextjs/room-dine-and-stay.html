The Dine and Stay package is a celebration of culinary indulgence combined with restful luxury. This 38-square-meter suite includes a private dining arrangement and curated meal credits to enjoy throughout your stay, all within a beautifully appointed room featuring a lush garden view.

Every detail of this residence has been considered with extraordinary care — from the hand-stitched linens to the custom-designed furniture, from the curated art on the walls to the panoramic views beyond the floor-to-ceiling windows. Staying here is not merely accommodation; it is an experience that redefines the very notion of comfort.

Our dedicated in-room dining service operates around the clock, bringing the flavors of our award-winning restaurant directly to your door. Whether you prefer a leisurely breakfast on your private terrace or a late-night tasting of our chef's seasonal creations, we are here to accommodate every preference.
