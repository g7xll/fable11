FAQ

Your Questions, Answered

Everything you need to know about FinProX. Can't find an answer? Our team is happy to help.

Is my financial data secure with FinProX? +
Absolutely. FinProX uses 256-bit AES encryption for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. We are SOC 2 Type II certified and undergo annual penetration testing. Your data is never sold or shared with third parties.
Can I connect multiple bank accounts and payment processors? +
Yes. FinProX Pro and Enterprise plans support unlimited account connections via our direct bank integrations and Plaid-powered connectivity. We support over 12,000 financial institutions globally, plus Stripe, PayPal, Square, and more.
How does the free plan work? Is there a credit card required? +
Our Starter plan is completely free and requires no credit card to sign up. You get access to basic cash flow dashboards, up to 3 connected accounts, and monthly summaries. Upgrade any time when you're ready for more features.
Can I export reports to share with my accountant? +
Yes. You can export all financial reports as PDF, CSV, or Excel files. Pro and Enterprise plans also include an accountant collaboration portal with view-only access and direct commenting, making tax season significantly easier.
What happens if I want to cancel my subscription? +
You can cancel at any time with no penalties or lock-in periods. When you cancel, your account reverts to the free Starter plan. You'll retain access to all your historical data and can export everything before or after cancellation.
