TechFlow Inc. Reduces Manual Work by 60%

Industry FinTech
Year 2026
Result 60% time saved

The Challenge

TechFlow Inc., a fast-growing FinTech company with 200–500 employees, was struggling under the weight of manual financial processes. Their finance team spent over 30 hours per week reconciling spreadsheets, manually entering data from multiple banking platforms, and generating monthly reports by hand. As the company scaled, these inefficiencies compounded — errors crept into reports, close cycles stretched past 10 business days, and the team had no real-time view of cash position.

Leadership needed a solution that could eliminate repetitive manual work, integrate with existing banking and ERP systems, and provide the finance team with accurate, up-to-the-minute financial data. The status quo was not just slowing them down — it was introducing material risk into their financial reporting.

The Solution

TechFlow partnered with FinProX to implement an end-to-end financial management platform across their finance and accounting teams. The rollout began with automated bank feeds and reconciliation — eliminating the most labor-intensive manual tasks first. Within two weeks, daily bank reconciliation went from a 4-hour manual process to a 15-minute review of auto-matched transactions.

The team then configured custom dashboards for real-time cash flow visibility, accounts receivable aging, and spend analytics by department. FinProX's automated reporting engine replaced their monthly manual report builds, cutting report preparation time from three days to under two hours. Integration with their existing ERP ensured a single source of truth across systems.

"FinancePro helped us reduce manual work by 60% and gain clear insights into our cash flow. It's become essential to our operations." — Marvin McKinney, Finance Manager

The Results

  • 60% reduction in manual finance work within the first 90 days
  • $2M in annual savings from reduced labor costs and error remediation
  • Full return on investment achieved within 6 months of deployment
  • 99.9% data accuracy across all financial reporting and reconciliation
