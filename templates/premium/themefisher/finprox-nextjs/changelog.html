FAQ

Your questions, answered

Get quick answers to the most common questions about our platform and services.

What is the purpose of this platform?+

Our platform is designed to provide users with comprehensive tools and resources to enhance their productivity and collaboration.

How can I create an account?+

You can create an account by clicking the Sign Up button at the top of the page and filling out the registration form with your details.

Is there a free trial available?+

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial on all plans. No credit card required. You can explore all features before deciding on a plan.

How do I reset my password?+

Click the "Forgot Password" link on the sign-in page. Enter your email address and we'll send you a password reset link within a few minutes.

Where can I find support if I encounter issues?+

You can reach our support team via the Contact page, by emailing support@finprox.com, or by using the in-app chat widget available 24/7.
