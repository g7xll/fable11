What is the purpose of this platform?

FinProx is a comprehensive financial management platform designed to help businesses of all sizes track cash flow, generate reports, and gain real-time visibility into their finances — all from one unified dashboard.

How can I create an account?

Creating an account is simple. Click the "Get Started" button on any pricing card or visit our sign-up page. Fill in your name, email, and password, and you'll be up and running in under two minutes — no credit card required for the free trial.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for all new accounts. You get full access to Pro plan features during the trial with no credit card required. At the end of your trial, you can choose the plan that's right for you.

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click "Forgot password?" on the sign-in page. Enter your registered email address and we'll send you a secure reset link within a few minutes. The link expires after 24 hours for your security.

Where can I find support if I encounter issues?

Our support team is available via live chat, email, and phone. Pro and Enterprise customers also benefit from priority support with faster response times. You can also browse our comprehensive help center and community forums for self-service answers.
