1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us as well as data generated through your use of our Services. The types of information we may collect include:

  • Personal information — such as your name, email address, phone number, and billing details when you register for an account or subscribe to a plan.
  • Financial data — including account balances, transaction records, and budget information that you connect to or input into the platform.
  • Usage data — such as pages visited, features used, time spent on the platform, and actions taken within the Services.
  • Device information — including IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers collected automatically when you access our Services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to operate, maintain, and improve the Services, as well as to communicate with you and fulfill our legal obligations. Specifically, we use your information to:

  • Provide, personalize, and improve the Services, including generating financial reports and analytics tailored to your business.
  • Improve the platform through analysis of usage patterns and feedback to enhance features and user experience.
  • Send you transactional communications, product updates, security alerts, and, where you have opted in, marketing messages.
  • Comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes, and to enforce our Terms of Service.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the limited circumstances described below:

  • Service providers — We share data with trusted third-party vendors who assist us in operating the platform, such as cloud hosting providers, payment processors, and analytics services, under strict data processing agreements.
  • Legal requirements — We may disclose information when required to do so by law, court order, or government authority, or when we believe disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of FinProx, our users, or the public.
  • Business transfers — If FinProx is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email and/or a prominent notice on our website before your data is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

4. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our platform uses bank-level encryption (AES-256) for data at rest and TLS encryption for data in transit. We conduct regular security audits, maintain access controls, and train our staff on data protection best practices. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information. You can exercise these rights by contacting us at privacy@finprox.com:

  • Access your data — Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccuracies — Ask us to update or correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal information.
  • Delete your account — Request the deletion of your account and associated personal data, subject to certain legal retention requirements.
  • Export your data — Receive a portable copy of your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Opt out of marketing — Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email or by updating your account preferences.

6. Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to operate and improve the Services, remember your preferences, and analyze how users interact with our platform. Essential cookies are required for the Services to function properly and cannot be disabled. Analytics and preference cookies help us understand usage patterns and can be managed through your browser settings or our cookie preferences panel. By continuing to use our Services, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this section.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or the way we handle your personal information, please contact our Privacy team at privacy@finprox.com. We will respond to your inquiry within 30 days. For users in the European Economic Area, FinProx, Inc. acts as the data controller for your personal information.
