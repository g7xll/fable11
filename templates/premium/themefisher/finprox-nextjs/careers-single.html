About the Role

We're looking for a Senior Product Designer to join our growing design team and help shape the future of financial technology at FinProX. In this role, you'll be responsible for designing intuitive, beautiful, and accessible interfaces across our core product suite — from our analytics dashboard to our reporting tools and mobile experience.

You'll work closely with product managers, engineers, and leadership to define user experiences that make complex financial data understandable and actionable for businesses of all sizes. This is a high-impact individual contributor role with opportunities to lead and mentor as the team grows.

If you're passionate about design craft, user research, and building products that genuinely help people manage their finances better, we'd love to hear from you.

Responsibilities

  • Lead end-to-end design for key product areas, from discovery and ideation through to pixel-perfect execution and handoff
  • Conduct user research, usability testing, and competitive analysis to uncover insights and validate design decisions
  • Create wireframes, user flows, prototypes, and high-fidelity mockups using Figma
  • Collaborate with product managers to define requirements and translate them into clear, elegant design solutions
  • Partner with engineers to ensure accurate implementation and advocate for design quality throughout development
  • Contribute to and maintain our design system, ensuring consistency across all product surfaces

Requirements

  • 5+ years of product design experience, ideally in a B2B SaaS or fintech environment
  • A strong portfolio demonstrating end-to-end product design work, including complex data-heavy interfaces
  • Expert proficiency with Figma and modern design tooling
  • Experience conducting and synthesizing user research to inform design decisions
  • Strong understanding of interaction design principles, accessibility, and responsive design
  • Excellent communication skills — you can explain design decisions clearly to both designers and non-designers

Benefits

  • Health Insurance — comprehensive medical, dental, and vision coverage
  • Competitive Salary — we benchmark against top-of-market compensation
  • Unlimited PTO — take the time you need to recharge
  • Remote Flexibility — work from anywhere with flexible hybrid options
  • Learning Budget — $2,000 per year for courses, books, and conferences
  • Home Office Stipend — $1,500 to set up your ideal workspace
