FAQ

Your questions, answered

Get quick answers to the most common questions about our platform and services.

What integrations does FinProx support?+

FinProx integrates with 20+ tools including Stripe, PayPal, Shopify, Slack, Notion, Google Drive, GitHub, and many more. New integrations are added regularly based on customer demand.

How do I set up a new integration?+

Setting up an integration takes just a few clicks. Navigate to the Integrations page in your dashboard, select the tool you want to connect, and follow the on-screen authorization steps. Most integrations are live in under 2 minutes.

Is my data secure when connecting third-party tools?+

Yes. All integrations use OAuth 2.0 or API key-based authentication. We never store your third-party credentials, and all data in transit is encrypted with TLS 1.3. We are SOC 2 Type II certified.

Can I request a new integration?+

Absolutely. Submit integration requests via our feedback portal or contact our support team. We prioritize integrations based on the number of requests from our user community.

Do integrations sync in real time?+

Most integrations sync data in real time or near-real time. Payment integrations like Stripe and PayPal push events instantly via webhooks. Cloud storage integrations sync on a scheduled basis (every 15 minutes by default).
