Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any billing differences.

If you cancel your subscription, your data will be retained for 30 days, during which you can export it. After that period, it will be permanently deleted from our servers.

Yes! On the Pro and Enterprise plans, you can add unlimited team members. Each additional member gets their own login and customizable permission levels.
