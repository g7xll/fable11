About FinProX

The story behind our mission to simplify financial management for businesses everywhere.

Our Mission

We Believe Great Finance Tools Should Be For Everyone

FinProX was founded in 2020 with a single conviction: world-class financial tools shouldn't be reserved for large enterprises with dedicated CFOs. Every business — from a solo freelancer to a high-growth startup — deserves real-time visibility into their finances, beautiful reporting, and AI-powered forecasting.

We started by talking to hundreds of founders who were drowning in spreadsheets, missing invoices, and scrambling to prepare investor updates. Today, FinProX serves over 10,000 businesses across 50+ countries — and we're just getting started.
