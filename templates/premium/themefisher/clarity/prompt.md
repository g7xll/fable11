## SUMMARY

CLARITY IS A MODERN SAAS/SECURITY MARKETING TEMPLATE WITH A CLEAN WHITE BACKGROUND, PURPLE ACCENT COLOR (#A97EFF), SATOSHI BODY FONT, AND CLASH GROTESK HEADING FONT. THE TEMPLATE FEATURES A FIXED NAVBAR WITH A MEGA DROPDOWN FOR "PAGES", A HERO SECTION WITH ANIMATED SVG BACKGROUND, BRAND MARQUEE, FEATURE HIGHLIGHTS, PRICING PLANS WITH TOGGLE, CUSTOMER REVIEWS CAROUSEL, AND A CALL-TO-ACTION SECTION. ALL PAGES SHARE THE SAME HEADER AND FOOTER. THE DESIGN IS RESPONSIVE WITH MOBILE HAMBURGER MENU AND USES AOS (ANIMATE ON SCROLL) FOR ENTRANCE ANIMATIONS.
