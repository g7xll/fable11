General Questions

Clarity is a comprehensive web security platform that protects your business from evolving cyber threats. Our platform combines real-time threat detection, automated response, and intelligent analytics to keep your digital assets safe. Once integrated, Clarity continuously monitors your infrastructure and responds to threats before they cause damage.

Clarity is built for businesses of all sizes — from startups protecting their first product to enterprises managing complex multi-cloud environments. Our flexible plans and intuitive interface make enterprise-grade security accessible to any team, regardless of technical expertise.

Security & Compliance

Clarity detects a wide spectrum of threats including SQL injection, cross-site scripting (XSS), DDoS attacks, malware, ransomware, unauthorized access attempts, data exfiltration, and zero-day exploits. Our AI engine continuously learns from new attack patterns to stay ahead of emerging threats.

Absolutely. All data in transit is protected with TLS 1.3, and data at rest is encrypted with AES-256. We follow a zero-knowledge architecture for sensitive credentials, meaning even our engineers cannot access your private keys or passwords.

Clarity helps you maintain compliance with SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, GDPR, HIPAA, and PCI DSS. Our automated compliance reporting generates audit-ready documentation and continuous evidence collection, dramatically reducing the burden of compliance reviews.
