## SUMMARY

ANDROMEDA IS A SAAS/PRODUCT LANDING PAGE TEMPLATE BY THEMEFISHER BUILT WITH NEXT.JS. IT FEATURES AN ORANGE-ACCENTED CLEAN WHITE DESIGN WITH POPPINS FONT, DECORATIVE FLOATING CIRCLES, AOS SCROLL ANIMATIONS, AND SWIPER CAROUSELS. THE TEMPLATE INCLUDES 13 PAGES: HOME, ABOUT, HOW IT WORKS, BLOG, CAREERS, CASE STUDIES, PRICING, SIGN IN, ELEMENTS, CHANGELOG, TERMS AND CONDITIONS, CONTACT, AND SIGN UP.
