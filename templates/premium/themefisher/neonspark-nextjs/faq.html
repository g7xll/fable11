Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services, pricing, and how we work. Can't find an answer? Get in touch.

General

What services does Neonspark offer?

Neonspark offers a full suite of digital services including web design, UI/UX design, copywriting, digital marketing, SEO optimisation, and project management. We tailor every engagement to your specific goals, whether you are launching a new product, refreshing a brand, or scaling an existing digital presence.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A simple landing page typically takes 2–3 weeks, while a full website redesign or SaaS product build can range from 6 to 16 weeks. We provide a detailed project timeline during the discovery phase so you always know what to expect.

Do you work with startups as well as established businesses?

Absolutely. We work with early-stage startups, scale-ups, and enterprise organisations alike. Our engagement models are flexible enough to meet you where you are — whether that is a lean MVP build or a large-scale platform overhaul. We adapt our process to match your budget, timeline, and goals.

Pricing

How much does a website design project cost?

Project costs depend on scope, number of pages, integrations required, and the level of custom design. Our plans start at $99/user/month for the Professional tier and $199/user/month for Enterprise. For bespoke project engagements, we provide a fixed-price proposal after an initial discovery call — so there are no surprise invoices.

Do you offer refunds or a money-back guarantee?

We offer a 14-day satisfaction guarantee on our subscription plans. For project-based engagements, we structure work into milestones with client sign-off at each stage — if you are unhappy at any milestone, we will revise until you are satisfied or discuss a fair resolution. We are committed to delivering work you love.

Technical

Which technologies and platforms do you build with?

Our team is platform-agnostic. We work with Next.js, React, Vue, and Astro on the frontend; Node.js, Python, and Laravel on the backend; and WordPress, Webflow, or Sanity CMS for content-driven sites. We recommend the stack that best fits your performance, scalability, and maintenance requirements rather than defaulting to a single tool.

Will my website be optimised for search engines?

Yes. Every project we deliver includes on-page SEO foundations: semantic HTML structure, meta tags, Open Graph markup, sitemap generation, robots.txt configuration, Core Web Vitals optimisation, and accessibility compliance. For clients who want ongoing SEO strategy and content — keyword research, link building, technical audits — we offer a dedicated SEO retainer service.
