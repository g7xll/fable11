Questions Answered

Frequently asked questions

Answers to Common Questions About Our Mission and Work. Quick Guide to Our Programs and Processes.

How secure is my financial data on your platform?
We use industry-standard SSL encryption and secure payment processors to ensure all financial data is protected. Your personal and financial information is never shared with third parties without your consent.
Do you offer phone support?
Yes, we offer phone support during regular business hours. You can reach our team at our contact number listed on the contact page, and we'll be happy to assist you with any questions or concerns.
Can I use my own domain?
Absolutely! You can use this template with your own custom domain. Simply configure your DNS settings and point your domain to your hosting provider where you've deployed this template.
Do you offer a discount for annual plans?
Yes, we offer special discounts for organizations that commit to annual donation plans. These recurring contributions help us plan better and maximize our impact. Contact us for details.
Can I change my password?
Yes, you can change your password at any time through your account settings. If you've forgotten your password, we also provide a password reset option via email.
Do you offer a free plan?
We believe everyone should have the opportunity to contribute. We offer various giving levels including small monthly donations that can make a big collective difference. No minimum amount required.
