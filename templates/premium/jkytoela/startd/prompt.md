## SUMMARY

STARTD IS A SINGLE-PAGE SAAS/STARTUP LANDING PAGE TEMPLATE. IT USES A CLEAN WHITE CANVAS WITH INDIGO/BLUE ACCENTS, LARGE BOLD SANS-SERIF HEADLINES, SOFT GRAY GRADIENT SECTION BACKGROUNDS, AND A DARK NAVY "CASE STUDIES" BAND WITH AN ANIMATED PARTICLE FIELD. THE PAGE COVERS: NAV + HERO WITH A "WATCH THE VIDEO" PLAY BUTTON OVER AN ILLUSTRATION, A LOGO STRIP, A 3-STEP "TRANSFORM YOUR BUSINESS" LIST, A 6-CARD FEATURE GRID, A DARK CASE-STUDY SHOWCASE, A TWO-TESTIMONIAL CAROUSEL (PREV/NEXT ARROWS), A PRICING/CTA BAND, AND A FOOTER WITH A NEWSLETTER FORM. THE ONLY IN-TEMPLATE NAVIGATION IS TO ANCHORS ON THIS SAME PAGE (ALL NAV/FOOTER LINKS POINT BACK TO `/`) — THERE ARE NO SEPARATE SUB-PAGES.
