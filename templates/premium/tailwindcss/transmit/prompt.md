# TRANSMIT — TAILWIND PLUS PODCAST TEMPLATE — STUDY / CLONE

> A SELF-CONTAINED, PIXEL-FAITHFUL REPRODUCTION OF THE TAILWIND PLUS "TRANSMIT" PODCAST TEMPLATE, REBUILT AS PLAIN HTML + CSS + VANILLA JS WITH NO BUILD STEP. EVERY PAGE, SECTION, HOVER STATE, AND THE STICKY AUDIO PLAYER ARE RECREATED FROM RECON ARTIFACTS. ALL ASSETS (THE SATOSHI FONT FAMILY AND THE "THEIR SIDE" COVER ART) ARE VENDORED LOCALLY SO THE CLONE RUNS OFFLINE. THIS IS A STUDY OF SOMEONE ELSE'S DESIGN, BUILT FOR LEARNING — ALL CREDIT FOR THE ORIGINAL GOES TO TAILWIND PLUS (TAILWIND LABS).

REFERENCE: `https://tailwindcss.com/plus/templates/transmit/preview`

## SUMMARY

TRANSMIT IS A PREMIUM PODCAST TEMPLATE FROM TAILWIND PLUS. THE DEMO CONTENT IS A FICTIONAL SHOW — **"THEIR SIDE"** ("CONVERSATIONS WITH THE MOST TRAGICALLY MISUNDERSTOOD PEOPLE OF OUR TIME"), HOSTED BY **ERIC GORDON / WES MANTOOTH** — THAT INTERVIEWS FAMOUS FICTIONAL VILLAINS (SKELETOR, HANK SCORPIO, THE WET BANDITS, SHOOTER MCGAVIN, BILL LUMBERGH). THE AESTHETIC IS A CLEAN, EDITORIAL, LIGHT (`slate-50`/WHITE) LAYOUT ON THE SATOSHI GROTESQUE FACE WITH A MONO META FONT, A PINK (`pink-500`) ACTION ACCENT, AND A SIGNATURE BLUE→VIOLET→PINK GRADIENT **AUDIO-WAVEFORM** SVG STRIP THAT RUNS ACROSS THE TOP OF THE MAIN COLUMN.

THE LIVE TEMPLATE IS A NEXT.JS APP SERVED FROM `transmit.tailwindui.com`; THIS CLONE PRESERVES ITS EXACT RENDERED MARKUP AND COMPILED TAILWIND CSS, SELF-HOSTS THE SATOSHI 400/500/700 WOFF2 WEIGHTS, VENDORS THE COVER ART LOCALLY, AND REPLACES THE NEXT + REACT-ARIA RUNTIME WITH A SMALL VANILLA-JS SHIM THAT REIMPLEMENTS THE SAME BEHAVIOURS (THE STICKY HTML5 `<audio>` PLAYER WITH PLAY/PAUSE, REWIND/FORWARD 10S, SCRUB SLIDER, TIME OUTPUT, PLAYBACK-RATE AND MUTE; HOVER/FOCUS STATES; "SHOW MORE" ABOUT TOGGLE).

THE BUILD SPANS **6 PAGES** — THE **HOME** EPISODE INDEX AND **5 EPISODE DETAIL PAGES** (`/1`–`/5`), ALL SHARING ONE PERSISTENT LEFT SIDEBAR (COVER, SHOW TITLE/BLURB, ABOUT, LISTEN LINKS, VERTICAL "HOSTED BY" RAIL) AND ONE STICKY BOTTOM AUDIO PLAYER.

### SPACING / SHAPE
- LAYOUT: FIXED LEFT SIDEBAR `lg:w-md xl:w-120` (`28rem`/`30rem`), MAIN COLUMN OFFSET `lg:ml-112 xl:ml-120`; CONTENT CENTERED `lg:max-w-4xl` / `md:max-w-2xl`, GUTTERS `px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8`
- RADII: COVER `rounded-2xl`; PLAY BUTTONS FULLY ROUND `rounded-full`; CARDS / MENU `rounded-lg`/`rounded-md`; SCRUB BAR `rounded-full`

- SIDEBAR HAS `lg:border-x lg:border-slate-200`; VERTICAL "HOSTED BY" RAIL USES `[writing-mode:vertical-rl]`
- WAVEFORM: SVG `h-20 w-full` AT TOP OF MAIN, A 400px-WIDE REPEATING `<pattern>` OF ~100 2px BARS AT VARYING HEIGHTS

### ANIMATION / EASINGS / INTERACTION
- DEFAULT TAILWIND TRANSITION `.15S CUBIC-BEZIER(.4,0,.2,1)`; HOVER COLOR SHIFTS ON NAV/LISTEN LINKS (`pink-500→pink-700`), LISTEN-PROVIDER ICONS (`slate-400→slate-600`), PLAY BUTTONS (`slate-700→slate-900`)
- **STICKY AUDIO PLAYER** — CLICKING ANY "LISTEN" / PLAY BUTTON SPAWNS A `fixed inset-x-0 bottom-0` BAR (`bg-white/90 backdrop-blur ring-slate-900/5`) WITH: PLAY/PAUSE TOGGLE, EPISODE TITLE LINK, REWIND-10S, FAST-FORWARD-10S, A SCRUB SLIDER (`bg-slate-100` TRACK, `bg-slate-700` FILL + THUMB), CURRENT/TOTAL TIME (`font-mono`), PLAYBACK-RATE CYCLE (1×/1.5×/2×), AND MUTE. DRIVEN BY A REAL HTML5 `<audio>` ELEMENT.
- **ABOUT "SHOW MORE"** — THE ABOUT BLURB IS `line-clamp-4`; "SHOW MORE" EXPANDS IT
- **FOCUS RINGS** — PLAY BUTTONS `focus:ring-3 ring-slate-700 ring-offset-4`; SCRUB / SMALL CONTROLS `focus:ring-2`

## LAYOUT & STRUCTURE

### SHARED CHROME
- **LEFT SIDEBAR** (`<header>`, FIXED ON `lg`) — VERTICAL "Hosted by / Eric Gordon · Wes Mantooth" RAIL (writing-mode vertical); "THEIR SIDE" COVER ART (links home); SHOW TITLE "Their Side" + BLURB "Conversations with the most tragically misunderstood people of our time."; **ABOUT** SECTION (violet/pink marker, clamped paragraph + "Show more"); **LISTEN** SECTION (indigo/blue marker, provider icon links: Spotify · Apple Podcast · Overcast · RSS Feed). ON SMALL SCREENS THE ABOUT + HOSTED-BY MOVE TO A BOTTOM `<footer>` AND LISTEN MOVES INLINE.
- **WAVEFORM HEADER** — GRADIENT-MASKED SVG BAR STRIP AT THE TOP OF THE MAIN COLUMN ON EVERY PAGE.
- **STICKY AUDIO PLAYER** — APPEARS AT THE BOTTOM OF THE MAIN COLUMN ONCE AN EPISODE IS PLAYED (see interaction above).

### PAGES
1. **HOME** (`/`) — "Episodes" H1, THEN A DIVIDED LIST OF 5 `<article>` ENTRIES (NEWEST FIRST: 5→1). EACH: MONO DATE EYEBROW, EPISODE TITLE LINK (`N: Name`), ONE-LINE DESCRIPTION, AND A "▶ Listen / Show notes" ACTION ROW. THE "LISTEN" BUTTON STARTS THAT EPISODE IN THE STICKY PLAYER; "SHOW NOTES" LINKS TO `/N`.
2–6. **EPISODE PAGES** (`/1`–`/5`) — SHARED ARTICLE LAYOUT: A LARGE ROUND PLAY BUTTON (`h-18 w-18 bg-slate-700`) BESIDE THE EPISODE TITLE (`text-4xl`) + MONO DATE; THE EPISODE DESCRIPTION; AN `<hr>`; THEN A PROSE BODY WITH MARKER-DOT H2 SECTIONS — **Topics**, **Sponsors**, **Links** (bulleted lists with en-dash markers). THE 5 EPISODES:

   - `/3` — **3: The Wet Bandits** (February 10, 2022)
