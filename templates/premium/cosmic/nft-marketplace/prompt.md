## SUMMARY

UNFT MARKETPLACE IS A COSMIC CMS + NEXT.JS + STRIPE DEMO STOREFRONT FOR BUYING AND SELLING DIGITAL ART / NFT-STYLE COLLECTIBLES. IT SHIPS A MARKETING HOME PAGE (HERO, "HOT BID" CAROUSEL, CATEGORY COLLECTIONS, A FEATURED-PRODUCT SPOTLIGHT, AN INTRODUCTION BANNER, A REVIEWS STRIP, AND A FULL "DISCOVER" GRID WITH FILTER CHIPS), AN ABOUT PAGE, A DEDICATED SEARCH/FILTER PAGE, A PER-ITEM PRODUCT DETAIL PAGE (REPEATED FOR EVERY LISTING IN THE CATALOG), AND AN "UPLOAD-DETAILS" / CREATE-ITEM PAGE THAT IS GATED BEHIND A COSMIC AUTHENTICATION MODAL. THE SITE ALREADY SHIPS A WORKING LIGHT/DARK THEME TOGGLE IN THE HEADER AND FOOTER.

  - PRICE-TAG GREEN: `#45b26b`
  - ALERT/PINK ACCENT: `#ef466f` / `#e41446`
  - FOOTER BACKGROUND: `#1a10b1` (deep indigo/blue)
- **PALETTE (DARK)**:
  - BACKGROUND: `#141416` (page), SURFACE: `#23262f`
  - BORDER/DIVIDER: `#353945`
  - HEADING TEXT: `#fcfcfd`, BODY TEXT: `#777e90`
  - ACCENT COLORS UNCHANGED (`#4437ff`, `#45b26b`, `#ef466f`)
  - FOOTER BACKGROUND: `#23262f` (dark mode swaps the footer to the dark surface tone rather than the indigo)
- **TYPE SCALE**: HERO H1 ~48px/1.15 BOLD, SECTION H2 ~32-36PX BOLD, CARD TITLE 16PX/500, BODY 14-16PX/400, LABEL/EYEBROW 12PX UPPERCASE LETTER-SPACED.
- **RADII**: CARDS/IMAGES 16PX, BUTTONS/PILLS FULLY ROUNDED (999PX), INPUTS 12-16PX, MODAL 24PX.
- **SHADOWS**: SOFT, LOW-OPACITY DROP SHADOWS ON CARDS AND THE MODAL (`0 4px 20px rgba(0,0,0,.08)`-STYLE).
- **ANIMATION/EASING**: CARD HOVER OVERLAY CONTROLS FADE IN VIA `opacity`/`visibility` OVER `all .2s` EASE; BUTTONS TRANSITION `background-color`/`border-color`/`color` OVER ~0.2S EASE; MOBILE MENU AND MODAL ARE SHOW/HIDE (DISPLAY FLEX) TOGGLES, NO COMPLEX KEYFRAME CHOREOGRAPHY.
- **THEME**: DRIVEN BY CSS CUSTOM PROPERTIES ON `:root` / `:root.dark`, TOGGLED BY THE HEADER/FOOTER SWITCH, PERSISTED TO `localStorage`, AND DEFAULTED FROM `prefers-color-scheme` ON FIRST LOAD (NO-FLASH BOOT SCRIPT).
