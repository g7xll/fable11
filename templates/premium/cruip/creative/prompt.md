## SUMMARY

CREATIVE IS A LIGHT-THEMED MARKETING LANDING PAGE TEMPLATE FOR A CREATIVE-INSPIRATION / MOOD-BOARD COMMUNITY PRODUCT ("JOIN THE COMMUNITY"). THE LIVE PREVIEW SERVES THE TEMPLATE FROM `https://preview.cruip.com/creative/` AS FOUR STATIC HTML PAGES. THE PREVIEW HOST'S OWN CHROME (TOP TOOLBAR WITH LOGO, VIEWPORT TOGGLES, "BUY NOW" BUTTON) IS NOT PART OF THE TEMPLATE AND WAS EXCLUDED.

## STYLE

- FONTS: "CABINET GROTESK" (SELF-HOSTED WOFF/WOFF2, WEIGHTS 500/700/800) FOR HEADLINES, "INTER" (GOOGLE FONTS, WEIGHTS 400/500/600) FOR BODY TEXT.
- PALETTE: SLATE/GRAY NEUTRALS (`#0f172a`-ISH DARK NAVY FOR HEADINGS AND DARK SECTIONS, `#f8fafc`-ISH LIGHT GRAY SECTION BACKGROUNDS, WHITE CARDS) WITH A SINGLE BLUE ACCENT (`#2563eb`-ISH) FOR LINKS, PRIMARY BUTTONS, AND THE "MINDS" HIGHLIGHT WORD IN THE HERO.
- TYPE SCALE: HERO H1 ~48-56PX EXTRABOLD, SECTION HEADINGS ~32-36PX BOLD, BODY ~16PX, SMALL/META TEXT ~14PX.
- RADII: FULLY ROUNDED (PILL) BUTTONS AND NAV LINKS; LARGE (~24-32PX) ROUNDED CORNERS ON CARDS, IMAGES, AND THE HERO CONTAINER; SMALL (~8PX) RADII ON FORM INPUTS.
- SHADOWS: SOFT, LOW-OPACITY DROP SHADOWS ON CARDS AND THE PRICING "MOST POPULAR" PLAN.
- ANIMATION: AOS ("ANIMATE ON SCROLL") LIBRARY DRIVES FADE-UP/FADE-RIGHT ENTRANCE ANIMATIONS ON HERO COPY, SECTION HEADINGS, AND GRID CARDS AS THEY SCROLL INTO VIEW (EASE-OUT-QUAD-STYLE TIMING, ~120-700MS DELAYS STAGGERED PER ELEMENT). SWIPER.JS DRIVES THE HORIZONTALLY-SCROLLING "TRENDING COLLECTIONS" AND "FROM OUR BLOG" CAROUSELS. ALPINE.JS DRIVES THE MOBILE HAMBURGER MENU TOGGLE AND ANY `x-show`/`x-data` INTERACTIVE WIDGETS. BUTTONS AND LINKS HAVE SIMPLE COLOR/OPACITY HOVER TRANSITIONS.
- NO DARK MODE IN THE SOURCE TEMPLATE — IT IS A FIXED LIGHT DESIGN, SO THE CLONE REPRODUCES IT AS LIGHT-ONLY TO STAY FAITHFUL TO THE ORIGINAL.
