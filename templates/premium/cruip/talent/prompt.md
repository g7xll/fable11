## SUMMARY

TALENT IS A CLEAN, PROFESSIONAL SAAS / TALENT MARKETPLACE LANDING PAGE TEMPLATE FROM CRUIP. IT CONSISTS OF FOUR PAGES: A COMPREHENSIVE HOME PAGE, A SCHEDULE-A-CALL FORM PAGE, A SIGN-IN PAGE, AND A RESET-PASSWORD PAGE. THE DESIGN USES A PREDOMINANTLY WHITE/LIGHT BACKGROUND WITH BLUE ACCENTS (BLUE-600 / #2563EB) AND DARK NAVY SECTIONS. TYPOGRAPHY USES ASPEKTA FOR BODY TEXT AND CABINET GROTESK FOR HEADINGS AND BUTTONS. ANIMATIONS ARE POWERED BY AOS (ANIMATE ON SCROLL) AND SWIPER.JS FOR CAROUSELS.
