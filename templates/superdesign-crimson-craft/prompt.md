### SPEC

CREATE A DESIGN WITH A BACKGROUND OF #121212 AND AN ACCENT COLOR OF #E11D48. USE 'CAL SANS' FOR HEADERS WITH A FONT-WEIGHT OF 700-900 AND TIGHT LETTER-SPACING. USE 'POPPINS' FOR BODY COPY.
