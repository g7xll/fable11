## SUMMARY

NEXTMERCE IS A CLEAN, CONVERSION-FOCUSED ONLINE-ELECTRONICS-STORE TEMPLATE (THE PUBLIC DEMO OF THE NEXTMERCE NEXT.JS E-COMMERCE BOILERPLATE BY PIMJO). IT IS A FULL MULTI-PAGE STOREFRONT: A RICH HOME PAGE (HERO CAROUSEL, CATEGORY CIRCLES, NEW ARRIVALS, PROMO BANNERS, BEST SELLERS, A LIVE COUNTDOWN CTA, TESTIMONIALS, NEWSLETTER), SHOP LISTING PAGES (WITH AND WITHOUT FILTER SIDEBAR), CATEGORY AND POPULAR LISTINGS, A PRODUCT-DETAILS PAGE (GALLERY + TABS + RECENTLY-VIEWED CAROUSEL), CART, CHECKOUT, WISHLIST, A BLOG (GRID / GRID-WITH-SIDEBAR / DETAILS / DETAILS-WITH-SIDEBAR), CONTACT, SIGN-IN, SIGN-UP, A MAIL-SUCCESS CONFIRMATION, AND A 404 ERROR PAGE. THE DESIGN LANGUAGE IS BRIGHT AND TRUSTWORTHY: WHITE SURFACES, A DEEP NAVY INK (#1C274C), A SIGNATURE INDIGO-BLUE PRIMARY (#3C50E0), SOFT GRAY SECTION BANDS, GENEROUS ROUNDED CORNERS AND SUBTLE CARD SHADOWS.

## STYLE

THE STYLE IS MODERN SAAS-COMMERCE: AIRY, GRID-DRIVEN, FRIENDLY. TYPOGRAPHY USES THE TEMPLATE'S ACTUAL RENDERED FONT STACK — A SYSTEM SANS-SERIF (`ui-sans-serif, system-ui, sans-serif, ...`) — WITH NAVY HEADINGS (BOLD/SEMIBOLD) AND A MUTED SLATE-GRAY BODY (#6C6F93). PRIMARY ACTIONS ARE INDIGO-BLUE (#3C50E0) PILLS OR NAVY (#1C274C) PILLS; LINKS AND ACTIVE STATES GLOW BLUE. CARDS HAVE WHITE FILLS, ~10PX RADII, AND A SOFT SHADOW THAT DEEPENS ON HOVER. PRODUCT IMAGES SCALE GENTLY ON HOVER WHILE A FLOATING ACTION BAR (WISHLIST / QUICK-VIEW / ADD-TO-CART) SLIDES OR FADES IN. MOTION IS RESTRAINED: 200–400MS EASE TRANSITIONS ON HOVER, FADE-UP REVEALS, AND A LIVE COUNTDOWN TIMER.
