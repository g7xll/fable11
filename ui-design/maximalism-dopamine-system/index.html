What's inside

EVERY PIXEL SPARKS JOY

A complete system of clashing colors, layered textures and bouncy motion — engineered so nothing ever feels flat, quiet, or generic.

5-Accent Rotation

Magenta, cyan, yellow, orange and purple cycle through every section and grid by index modulo 5. The same color never dominates twice in a row.

Pattern-on-Pattern

Dots over stripes, checker over mesh — two global patterns plus section-specific layers, each at low opacity, stacking into rich, never-empty backgrounds.

Stacked Shadows

Never a single shadow. Soft colored glows combine with hard offset stacks that double their distance — 8px, 16px, 24px — in clashing accents.

Bouncy Motion

Float, wiggle, spin, pulse-glow and gradient-shift keep 30-40% of elements in continuous, GPU-accelerated motion. Nothing stays still.

Massive Type

Headlines from 72px to 128px with triple and mega text-shadow stacks, plus oversized words bleeding off the edges for depth and chaos.

Accessible Chaos

19.5:1 body contrast, double-ring focus states with dashed outlines, ARIA on every control, and a full prefers-reduced-motion fallback.
