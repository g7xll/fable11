01 · The physics

Four states of the same clay

Depth is the whole language. A light source sits top-left, a cool shadow falls bottom-right, and the alpha-blended pair carves every edge — no borders, no flat fills.

Extruded

The resting state. The element lifts off the surface, catching light on top-left.

9px · -9px · 16px blur

Inset

A shallow press. Shadows flip inward, so the element sinks into the clay.

inset 6px · -6px

Inset deep

A drilled well for inputs and icons — significantly deeper, with real 3D depth.

inset 10px · -10px

Nested

Extruded → inset → extruded. Stacking states is how the system shows off.

Cards with drilled-in icon wells
