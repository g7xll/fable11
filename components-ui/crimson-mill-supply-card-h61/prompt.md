> A SELF-CONTAINED GENERATION PROMPT FOR A SINGLE, VERTICAL, MOBILE-FIRST
> "DIGITAL VISITING CARD" — A TAP-TO-CONTACT BRAND PAGE FOR A HERITAGE
> STONE-MILL SUPPLIER OF FLOURS, GRAINS, AND SPICES. THE PAGE STACKS A
> BRANDED HERO, A TWO-UP CONTACT GRID, A PRODUCT SHOWCASE GALLERY, A
> FLAGSHIP-STORE LOCATION BLOCK, AND A HOURS/FOOTER STRIP — EACH AS A
> ROUNDED, SOFT-SHADOWED CARD ON A WARM CANVAS, REVEALED ON SCROLL.

## AESTHETIC IDENTITY

THE NAMED DESIGN LANGUAGE IS **"MILLSTONE CRAFT"** — A WARM, ARTISANAL,
PRINT-ADJACENT IDENTITY FOR A FICTIONAL HERITAGE BUSINESS NAMED
**CRIMSON MILL** ("STONE-GROUND SINCE 1962"). THE MOOD IS HONEST,
HAND-MADE, AND CONFIDENT — A BRANDED PACKAGING LABEL CROSSED WITH A
MODERN LINK-IN-BIO CARD. IT IS DELIBERATELY THE OPPOSITE OF GENERIC
SAAS / AI-SLOP: NO PURPLE GRADIENTS, NO GLASSMORPHISM, NO NEON. WARM
PAPER-CREAM CANVAS, INK-CHARCOAL TYPE, A DECISIVE BRICK-CRIMSON ACCENT,
AND A WHEAT-GOLD SECONDARY. CONDENSED UPPERCASE DISPLAY TYPE GIVES IT
THE FEEL OF A STAMPED FLOUR SACK.

## MOTION / ANIMATION / INTERACTION

- **SCROLL REVEAL**: EACH CARD STARTS AT `opacity:0` AND `translateY(16px)`
  AND EASES IN (`cubic-bezier(0.22,1,0.36,1)`, ~`0.8s`) VIA AN
  `IntersectionObserver` AS IT ENTERS THE VIEWPORT, WITH A SMALL
  STAGGER BETWEEN SIBLINGS.
- **MARQUEE TICKER**: HARDWARE-ACCELERATED `translateX` LOOP (~30S),
  DUPLICATED CONTENT FOR A SEAMLESS WRAP; PAUSES ON HOVER.
- **PRESS FEEDBACK**: CONTACT AND BUTTON CARDS SCALE TO `~0.96` ON
  ACTIVE; SUBTLE LIFT (TRANSLATE + SHADOW) ON HOVER.
- **LIVE DOT**: THE LOCATION "OPEN" DOT GENTLY PULSES WITH A SOFT GLOW.
- RESPECT `prefers-reduced-motion`: DISABLE THE MARQUEE AND REVEAL
  TRANSFORMS, SHOWING ALL CONTENT IMMEDIATELY.

## RESPONSIVE BEHAVIOR

- MOBILE-FIRST SINGLE COLUMN; COMFORTABLE TAP TARGETS (≥48PX).
- AT THE DESKTOP BREAKPOINT (~`768px`+): WIDEN THE COLUMN, MAKE THE
  SHOWCASE 4-UP, AND TURN THE LOCATION CARD HORIZONTAL WITH A FIXED
  ACTION RAIL. HERO TYPE SCALES UP.
- NO HORIZONTAL OVERFLOW; ANTIALIASED TEXT; SMOOTH SCROLL.
