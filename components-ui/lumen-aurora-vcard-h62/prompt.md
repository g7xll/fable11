# LUMEN AURORA — A CINEMATIC PERSONAL DIGITAL VISITING CARD

> A SELF-CONTAINED GENERATION PROMPT FOR A SINGLE-PAGE, MOBILE-FIRST
> PERSONAL "DIGITAL VISITING CARD" (vCARD) FOR AN INDIVIDUAL — THE KIND OF
> IDENTITY PAGE SOMEONE SHARES BY A QR CODE OR A LINK. THE PAGE CENTRES ONE
> TACTILE, FROSTED-GLASS CARD THAT FLOATS OVER A SLOW, LIVING AURORA OF
> COLOUR. IT STACKS: AN AVATAR + NAME + ROLE HERO WITH A LIVE "AVAILABLE"
> STATUS, A SHORT FIRST-PERSON BIO, A ROW OF QUICK CONTACT ACTIONS
> (EMAIL / CALL / WHATSAPP), A SOCIAL-LINK ROW, A "SAVE CONTACT" vCARD
> DOWNLOAD AFFORDANCE, AND A MINIMAL FOOTER WITH A LOCATION + LOCAL CLOCK.

READS LIKE AN EDITORIAL CALLING CARD, NOT A DASHBOARD.

A SINGLE CENTERED CARD, MAX-WIDTH ≈ 420px, VERTICALLY CENTERED IN THE
VIEWPORT, FLOATING OVER THE FULL-BLEED AURORA BACKGROUND. INSIDE THE
CARD, TOP TO BOTTOM:

1. **TOP META BAR** — A MONO ROW: LEFT, A SMALL "◦ LUMEN CARD" WORDMARK;
   RIGHT, A LIVE LOCAL CLOCK (HH:MM:SS, TICKING) WITH A TIMEZONE LABEL.
2. **HERO** — A CIRCULAR AVATAR (VENDORED PORTRAIT) WITH A THIN GRADIENT
   RING AND A SOFT GLOW. BESIDE / BELOW IT: THE NAME IN DISPLAY SERIF,
   THE ROLE IN GROTESK, AND A STATUS PILL: A PULSING EMERALD DOT +
   "AVAILABLE FOR WORK" IN MONO UPPERCASE.
3. **BIO** — ONE SHORT FIRST-PERSON PARAGRAPH (2–3 LINES) IN SECONDARY
   TEXT, PLUS A ROW OF 3 SMALL MONO "TAG" CHIPS (E.G. `WEBGL`,
   `CREATIVE CODE`, `ART DIRECTION`).
4. **QUICK ACTIONS GRID** — THREE EQUAL GLASS TILES IN A ROW, EACH WITH
   AN INLINE SVG ICON ABOVE A LABEL: **EMAIL** (`mailto:`), **CALL**
   (`tel:`), **WHATSAPP** (`https://wa.me/...`). REAL, WORKING LINKS.
5. **SOCIAL ROW** — A HAIRLINE-DIVIDED ROW OF 4–5 CIRCULAR ICON BUTTONS
   (X / TWITTER, GITHUB, DRIBBBLE, LINKEDIN, INSTAGRAM) WITH INLINE SVG
   GLYPHS; HOVER LIFTS + TINTS EACH.
6. **PRIMARY CTA** — A FULL-WIDTH **"SAVE CONTACT"** BUTTON IN SIGNAL
   AMBER WITH A DOWNLOAD ICON. ON CLICK IT GENERATES A vCARD 3.0 (`.vcf`)
   BLOB IN-BROWSER (NAME, ROLE/TITLE, EMAIL, TEL, URL, NOTE) AND TRIGGERS
   A DOWNLOAD VIA AN OBJECT URL — NO SERVER. SECONDARY GHOST BUTTON
   **"COPY LINK"** COPIES THE PAGE URL TO THE CLIPBOARD WITH A TOAST.
7. **FOOTER** — A MONO STRIP: LOCATION ("STOCKHOLM, SE"), A "MADE WITH
   LUMEN" CREDIT, AND A SMALL QR-CODE GLYPH HINT (DRAWN AS A CSS/SVG
   MINI QR MOTIF, DECORATIVE).

## MOTION / INTERACTION SPEC

- **AURORA**: 3–4 LARGE RADIAL-GRADIENT BLOBS DRIFT AND BREATHE ON A
  SLOW 18–26s LOOP (CSS `@keyframes` ON `transform: translate/scale` AND
  HUE), GIVING A LIVING BACKGROUND. A FINE FILM-GRAIN / NOISE OVERLAY
  (SVG `feTurbulence` OR A TILED PNG AT LOW OPACITY) SITS ABOVE IT TO
  KILL BANDING.
- **CARD ENTRANCE**: ON LOAD, THE CARD AND ITS CHILDREN STAGGER UP +
  FADE IN (TRANSLATEY 14px → 0, 60–80ms STAGGER), WITH A SOFT
  CUBIC-BEZIER EASE.
- **POINTER PARALLAX (DESKTOP)**: THE CARD TILTS A FEW DEGREES TOWARD THE
  CURSOR (`rotateX/rotateY`, MAX ±6°) AND A SPECULAR HIGHLIGHT FOLLOWS
  THE POINTER ACROSS THE GLASS. DISABLED ON TOUCH / COARSE POINTERS.
- **STATUS DOT**: AN EMERALD DOT WITH A REPEATING EXPANDING-RING PULSE.
- **HOVER**: ACTION TILES + SOCIAL BUTTONS LIFT (`translateY(-2px)`),
  BRIGHTEN THEIR BORDER, AND GAIN A SOFT GLOW; THE AMBER CTA WARMS AND
  ITS ICON NUDGES DOWN.
- **TOAST**: A SMALL GLASS TOAST SLIDES UP FROM THE BOTTOM ON
  SAVE-CONTACT ("CONTACT DOWNLOADED") AND COPY-LINK ("LINK COPIED").
- **CLOCK**: THE FOOTER/META CLOCK TICKS EVERY SECOND.
- RESPECT `prefers-reduced-motion`: DISABLE PARALLAX, DRIFT, AND PULSE,
  KEEPING A STATIC, LEGIBLE CARD.

## RESPONSIVE BEHAVIOR

- MOBILE-FIRST: BELOW 480px THE CARD IS NEAR FULL-WIDTH WITH COMFORTABLE
  PADDING; THE QUICK-ACTIONS STAY A 3-UP ROW BUT SHRINK GRACEFULLY.
- ON LARGER SCREENS THE CARD CAPS AT ~420px AND CENTERS, WITH THE AURORA
  FILLING THE REST OF THE VIEWPORT; PARALLAX ENABLES.
- TOUCH TARGETS ≥ 44px; FULLY KEYBOARD-FOCUSABLE WITH VISIBLE FOCUS
  RINGS; SUFFICIENT CONTRAST FOR ALL TEXT.
